1  Workshop Meeting
June 24, 2008

At the Workshop Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council held on June 24, 2008 at 7:00 pm in
the Council Chambers, Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox,
Virginia, there present were Mayor John L. Wilson, presiding and Council members Joyce B.
Bennett, N. H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr.  Absent were Council members
John T. “Plicky” Williams, Jennifer Jamerson-Scruggs, Steven E. Lawson.   Also in attendance
were Barbara R. Williams, Clerk of the Circuit Court, Benny Harvey, Lucy Harvey, Chad
Millner, Nina Beasley, Carlton Duck, Beckie Nix, Stephanie James, Tammy David, Paul Harvey
& family, Mr. and Mrs. Karl Carter, Bryan Baine, Sam Carter, Bob Hopkins, John Redding,
Leslie Austin, Frank A. Wright, Jr., Town Attorney, David T. Garrett, Jr., Town Manager and
Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk.

Mayor Wilson called the meeting to order.

Mayor Wilson cited that a quorum was not present.  Mayor Wilson explained §5-10 of the Code
of the Town of Appomattox, which would allow for the meeting to remain open for 30 minutes
while waiting for a quorum.

Mayor Wilson congratulated the new members of the council.  Mrs. Barbara R. Williams, Clerk
of the Circuit Court was present and administered the oath of office to the newly elected Mayor
and Council members present.

At 7:30 p.m., Mayor Wilson cited the next regular council meeting would be held on Monday,
July 14, 2008 at 7:30 p.m. and a Special Call of Council would be signed by him in the morning
for a meeting on Monday, June 30, 2008 at 7:00 p.m.

Mayor Wilson closed the meeting for a lack of a quorum.

Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk

John L. Wilson, Mayor
