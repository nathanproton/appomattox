Special Call Meeting
June 2, 2008

At the Special Call Council Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council held on June 2, 2008 at
5:30 pm in the Council Chambers, Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia, there present were Mayor John L. Wilson, presiding and Council
members Joyce B. Bennett, N. H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, John T. “Plicky” Williams, Steven E.
Lawson, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr., and Jennifer Jamerson-Scruggs.   Also in attendance were,
Karl Carter, David T. Garrett, Jr., Town Manager and Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk.

Mayor Wilson called the meeting to order.

On a motion by Mr. Williams, seconded by Mr. Lawson and with all members present voting
“aye”, Council approved to reschedule the Regular Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council
scheduled for Monday, June 9, 2008 at 7:30 p.m. to Monday, June 16, 2008 at 7:30 p.m.  Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. McDearmon, seconded by Ms. Jamerson-Scruggs and with all members
present voting “aye”, Council voted to adjourn at 5:50 p.m.  Motion carried.

Roxanne W. Paulette, CMC
Town Clerk

John L. Wilson,
Mayor
