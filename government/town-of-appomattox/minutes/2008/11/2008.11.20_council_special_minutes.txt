1  Special Call Meeting
November 20, 2008

At the Special Call Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council held on November 20, 2008 at
5:00 pm in the Council Chambers, Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia, there present were Mayor Paul D. Harvey, presiding and Council
members Bryan J. Baine, N. H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr., John T. “Plicky”
Williams and Karl E. Carter.   Also in attendance were David T. Garrett, Jr., Town Manager and
Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember Williams and with all
members present voting aye, Council voted to change the November Workshop from Monday,
November 24, 2008 at 7:00 p.m. to Tuesday, November 25, 2008 at 7:00 p.m.   Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. McDearmon and with all members present
voting “aye”, Council voted to adjourn at 5:02 p.m.  Motion carried.

Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk

Paul D. Harvey, Mayor
