On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council approved the 1985 slate of officers for the Appomattox Volunteer Fire Department and all members voting aye. Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle reported on the Forum planned for February 13, 1985, at 7:00 P.M. at the County Courthouse on the traffic problem on Route 460.

Council discussed at length the problem of parking on Church Street at Liberty Baptist Church. The letters from Dr. Scott and Mr. Harwood were presented.

Mr. Bass recommended to Council that a letter be written to Liberty Baptist Church explaining that the action taken by Council was done in good faith and with the safety of the citizens in mind. The Town Council recognizes that a hazardous situation does exist but if the church members are willing to live with this, then we will leave the problem in their hands.

Mr. Britton explained that he and Harold Nash are dissatisfied with some of the designs of the Lagoon. They are working it out with the Health Department.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:07 P.M.
