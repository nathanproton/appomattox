date: 1985-05-13
content: On a motion by Mr. Webb, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted to install a Street light on Church Street for Liberty Baptist Church payable in advance by the Church and increases by Virginia Power to be added accordingly.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Noble-aye Hubbard-aye Webb-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted in accordance with Section 2.1-344, of the Town Code, to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council reconvened into regular session and all voting aye. Motion carried.

The Council has accepted the report from the Board of Supervisors that after July 1, 1985, Mr. Britton's duties will be only Emergency Services Coordinator for 10% of his time. No action was taken at this time.

There being no further business to come before the Council the meeting adjourned.
