content: WATER & SEWER COMMITTEE MEETING

A combined Water and Sewer Committee meeting was held at 5:15 on Monday, June 20, 1977 at the Town Office.

The purpose of the meeting was to discuss a request from W. E. Jamerson for a water and sewer connection at the Triangle Plaza Shopping Center.

Mr. Spiggle made the motion, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, with all members voting aye, to recommend to Town Council that a sewer connection be granted for a new Revco store and the Ben Franklin Store.

Mr. Bass made the motion, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, with all members voting aye, to recommend to Council that permission be given for a sewer connection to the new Revco Store and the Ben Franklin Store.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned.

R. C. Spiggle — Chairman

R. L. (Dick) Bass — Chairman
