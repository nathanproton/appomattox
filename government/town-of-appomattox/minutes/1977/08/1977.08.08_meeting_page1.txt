WATER COMMITTEE

A meeting of the Water Committee was held at 5:30 P.M. on August 8, 1977, at the Town Office.

Members present were Mr. Bass, Mr. Guill, and Mr. Hubbard.

Others present were W. R. Britton, Jr. and Harold Nash, H. P. W.

On a motion by Guill, seconded by Mr. Hubbard and all members voting yes, to recommend that the revenue received would not justify incorporating Walton Place Subdivision Water System into the Appomattox Town Water System.

There being no further business the meeting adourned at 6:50.

R. L. Bass Chairman
