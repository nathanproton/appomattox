A special meeting of the Town met in the Mayor's office on Monday night March 28 at 8:00 P.M. with Mayor H.B. Pack presiding.

The following members were present, R.G. Hubbard, R.L. Bass, Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, and Dick Wingfield.

The meeting was called for the purpose of going over the maps of the Town and County as prepared by the State Planning Commission.

Signed H.B. Pack Mayor
Signed R.E. Hubbard Clerk

April Meeting

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's office, on April 18, 1960, at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor H.B. Pack presiding.

The following members were present, R.L. Bass, Burke Guill, Dick Wingfield, R.G. Hubbard, and Ivey Holland.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion made by R.C. Hubbard, second by Ivey Holland, that electric heat and lights be put in Town Manager's Office. Motion carried.

Signed H.B. Pack Mayor
Signed R.E. Hubbard Clerk
