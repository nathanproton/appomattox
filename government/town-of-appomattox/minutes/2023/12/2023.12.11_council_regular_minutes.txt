1  Appomattox Town Council
Regular Council Meeting
December 11, 2023

The Appomattox Town Council held a Regular Council meeting on Monday, December 11,
2023, at 6:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox,
Virginia with Mayor Conner, presiding.

Members present:  Jane T. Allen, James J. Boyce, Sr., Claudia G. Puckette, Mary Lou G.
Spiggle, and Timothy W. Garrett.

Absent:  Nathan A. Simpson

Others: Hailey Fox, Brown Edwards; Carlton Duck, Alive Media; Dana Glover, Kim Ray,
Treasurer; R. Terry McGhee, Town Manager; Roxanne W. Casto, Clerk of Council

Mayor Conner called the meeting to order at 6:30 p.m.

Reverend Duck led the prayer and Pledge of Allegiance to the Flag of the United States of
America.

Mayor Conner welcomed everyone to the meeting.  The Town has accomplished a lot this year.

Scheduled Public Appearances:
Hailey Fox with Brown Edwards appeared before the Town Council to provide an overview of
the final Fiscal Year 2023 Town Audit.

Adoption of the Consent Agenda:
On a motion by Mr. Garrett, seconded by Mrs. Puckette, the Town Council voted to adopt the
consent agenda as presented for December 11, 2023.  All members present voting aye.
Motion carried 5-0.

Unfinished Business:
None

None

Council Standing Committee Reports:

Physical Development Committee: none

Citizen Comment: none
