1  Workshop Meeting
    Tuesday, November 27, 2012

The Appomattox Town Council held a Workshop meeting on Tuesday, November 27,
2012 at 7:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present: Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Claudia G. Puckette.

Others present: Stephanie James (Times Virginian), Ben Cates (News & Advance),
Carlton Duck, Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer; C. William Gillespie, Interim Town
Manager; Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to Workshop meeting of November 27, 2012 to order.

Mr. Gillespie provided an update of the Council’s annual (3rd) retreat held on October 26,
2012.  Council reviewed six (6) areas that they will prioritize projects within for the next
12 months and also 24 months.  Staff will look at where funding is needed and what other
resources are also needed, if any.  The largest item needed is a capital improvement plan,
particularly water and sewer needs, exclusively within the Town.  Zoning, infrastructure
and economic incentives all connect together to make a master plan.

There will more discussion at the December 10, 2012 Council meeting and the final
document will be brought to Council for adoption at the January 14, 2013 Regular
Council meeting.

Committee Updates
Finance & Planning

Mr. McDearmon reported that the committee met on November 13, 2012 and reviewed
the revised zoning map, discussed participating in the Region 2000 Economic
Development Committee, discussed water and sewer revenue projections.  The
committee also discussed the renovations in the conference room and bathroom at the
Town Office.  The treasurer provided the committee information on on-line bill pay.
Other items discussed also included a request from the Appomattox Youth Association
regarding a donation for the lighting project and items needed at the Train Depot.

Physical Development Committee

Mr. Mayberry reported that the committee met today, November 27, 2012 and a lot of the
items discussed during the meeting were the items from the retreat that Mr. Gillespie
touched on at the beginning of the workshop meeting.  Mr. Mayberry commended the
staff and Council for the items completed throughout the previous year.

Mr. Gillespie commended Council.  Stating they were the easiest and most organized
council he has ever worked with.  Their willingness goes a long way in getting things
done.

Staff Updates

Town Manager – The zoning map is all but complete and so is the ordinance.  The clerk
stated there are about 1100 property owners that will need to be notified.  The process
will take some time.

The public works building is complete and will begin transitioning service requests,
public works needs all to that area in December 2012.  The new phone system is working
well.

The financial system (Logics) training is scheduled to begin in January 2013.

Treasurer – The FY 2012 field audit is complete.

Clerk of Council – Staff will be asking Council to cancel the December workshop during
the December Regular Meeting.  Offices will be closed December 24, 25, and 26, 2012.

Council Concerns

Mrs. Spiggle publicly thanked Rhonda Guill for all of her efforts for the Railroad Festival
and train excursion.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember McDearmon,
Council voted to adjourn at 7:18 p.m.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.
