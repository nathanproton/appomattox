1  Workshop Meeting
    Tuesday, February 28, 2012

The Appomattox Town Council held a Workshop meeting on Tuesday, February 28,
2012 at 7:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present: Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, and Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Joyce B. Bennett.

Others present:  Stephanie James, Cliff Harvey, Lucy Harvey, Benny Harvey, Carlton
Duck and Alive TV Crew, Shane Reid, John Reid, Wade Tanner, Stacey Wilkes, Town
Treasurer; C. William Gillespie, Interim Town Manager; Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk of
Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order.

Shane Reid, John Reid and Wade Tanner of Reid Engineering Company, Inc. provided an
in depth review of the Preliminary Engineering Report and Environmental Review
prepared for the Town of Appomattox regarding the Wastewater Project.

Council gave consensus to forward the ordinance outlining the creation of a Town
Planning Commission to legal counsel for review.

Mr. Gillespie reported on the organizational changes and Council provided consensus to
move forward with the changes outlined.  The Public Works department will begin
advertising for the part-time Administrative Assistant.  Council would like for the Clerk
to visit another locality in Virginia who is utilizing the Logics Software.  The general
fund would absorb 75% of the cost of the new software and the water fund would fund
the other 25%.  The Treasurer will present the total software cost and staff
recommendation at the regular March meeting.

Committee Updates
Finance & Planning – Mr. McDearmon reported the committee met on February 13, 2012
and discussed the following:

➢  An additional funding request from Sheriff Letterman.
➢  The FY 2013 Budget schedule was presented to the committee.
➢  Council presented an MOU to the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors for

the sewer line expansion along 460 West.

➢  An amendment to the Water and Sewer Accounts Receivable Policy
➢  Continued discussion of the Zoning Ordinances

Physical Development – Mr. Mayberry reported the committee met prior to the
Workshop meeting and received a Stormwater Management presentation from the
Department of Conservation and Recreation.

Staff Updates
Town Manager – Mr. Gillespie reported that he had met with Mr. Elder, Utility Director
and the Request for Proposals on the Public Works Buildings will be advertised on
Sunday, March 4th.  The proposals will be presented at the March 27, 2012 Physical
Development Committee meeting.

Mr. Gillespie also reported that the committee has not reported completely on the
blighted properties.

Treasurer – None

Clerk of Council – None

Council Concerns
Mayor Harvey recognized the Water and Wastewater Departments for their recent feature
in the nationally recognized TPO Magazine.  The Town will present each employee with
a plaque for recognition.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember Garrett,
Council voted to adjourn at 8:30.m.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.
