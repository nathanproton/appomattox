1  Workshop Meeting
    Tuesday, June 26, 2012

The Appomattox Town Council held a Workshop meeting on Tuesday, June 26, 2012 at
7:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox,
Virginia with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present: Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Joyce B. Bennett.

Others present:  Nina Beasley, Sam Carter, Chad Millner, Claudia Puckette, Stephanie
James, Carlton Duck and Alive TV Crew, Jeff Elder, Utility Director; Rhonda Guill,
Deputy Clerk; Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer; C. William Gillespie, Interim Town
Manager; Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order.

Joyce B. Bennett was recognized by the Mayor and Council Members with a plaque for
her service as Council Member – July 1, 2010 - June 30, 2012.  Mayor Harvey thanked
Ms. Bennett for her years of service and noted accomplishments.

Mr. Gillespie updated Council on discussions with the Appomattox County Board of
Supervisors regarding the sewer extension to the North Service Area of the County and
the proposed Boundary Line Adjustment.  The staff has been directed to work with the
County to conduct a VDOT style public hearing to provide facts, figures, maps, graphs,
proposed area of the boundary adjustment.

Mr. Gillespie provided an update on the activation of the 460 Waterline stating that Town
Staff and Water Department is to be commended for efforts in taking over the line.  We
are hoping to turn the line on July 12 or 13.  The operational plan has been submitted and
approved by CCUSA.  The citizens should not see any differences with pressure, etc.

Committee Updates
Finance & Planning – The committee met on June 11, 2012 and discussed the police
agreement and council gave consensus to leave the budget as presented, not including any
additional money above the HB599 funding.

Physical Development – The Committee met today, June 26, 2012.

Staff Updates
Town Manager – Mr. Gillespie spoke regarding the Virginia Autumn Special train
excursion planned for November 3, 2012 from Spencer, NC.  The Town will need
approximately 50 volunteers to coordinate the trip.

Treasurer – Reported that the Personal Property Tax program has been completely
reconciled.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember Garrett,
