1   Regular Council Meeting
     Monday, March 12, 2012

The Appomattox Town Council held a Regular Council Meeting on Monday, March 12,
2012 at 7:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia, with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present:  Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Joyce B. Bennett.

Others Present:  Cliff Harvey, Benny Harvey, Lucy Harvey, Rodney Longmire, Claudia
Puckette, Stephanie James, Carlton Duck and Alive TV Crew, Stacey Wilkes, Town
Treasurer; C. William Gillespie, Interim Town Manager and Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk
of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order.

Rev. Rodney Longmire led the invocation.

Rev. Rodney Longmire led the Pledge of Allegiance.

Mayor’s Comments
Mayor Harvey informed the Council and audience of the Tornado Preparedness Day –
March 20, 2012.  The siren erected behind Subway will sound as a test at 9:45 a.m. for
three minutes.

On a motion by Councilmember Garrett, seconded by Councilmember Mayberry,
Council voted to adopt the Consent Agenda for March 12, 2012.  All member present
voting aye.  Motion carried.

Unfinished Business
None

New Business

On a motion by Councilmember Garrett, seconded by Councilmember Bennett, Council
voted to accept the FY 2011 Audit Report from Brown, Edwards & Company, LLP.  All
members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

On a motion by Councilmember Spiggle, seconded by Councilmember McDearmon,
Council voted to authorize purchase of software programs for organizational restructuring
in the amount of $25,135.00. All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

On a motion by Councilmember Garrett, seconded by Councilmember Conner, Council
voted to declare the following items surplus equipment – 1980 Ford School Bus, 1987
GMC Diesel 7000 Dump Truck, 2003 Isuzu NQR Sweeper Truck, Hercules Leaf
Machine / Model 6800, John Deere 310 B Backhoe, Terex Rubber Tire Loader.  All
members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

2   Regular Council Meeting
     Monday, March 12, 2012

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember Bennett,
Council voted to approve the 2012 Slate of Officers for the Historic Appomattox
Railroad Festival.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

Roxanne Paulette, Clerk of Council gave an update on the Tourism Program and
Partnership Initiatives for “The Best of the Civil War…The End” grant.

Citizen Comment Period
None

Council Concerns
None

On a motion by Councilmember Garrett, seconded by Councilmember Mayberry,
Council voted to adjourn the meeting at 7:50 p.m.  All members present voting aye.
Motion carried.
