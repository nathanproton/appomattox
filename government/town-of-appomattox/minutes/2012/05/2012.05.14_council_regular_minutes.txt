1   Regular Council Meeting
     Monday, May 14, 2012

The Appomattox Town Council held a Regular Council Meeting on Monday, May 14,
2012 at 7:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia, with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present:  Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Joyce B. Bennett.

Others Present:  Lucy Harvey, Benny Harvey, Claudia Puckette, Jeff Elder, Utility
Director, Jim Gallegher (Our Lady of Peace Catholic church), Stephanie James, Carlton
Duck and Alive TV Crew, Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer; C. William Gillespie, Interim
Town Manager and Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order and welcomed everyone.

Father Gallegher, Our Lady of Peace Catholic Church led the invocation.

Father Gallegher also led the Pledge of Allegiance.

Mayor Harvey opened the public hearing.

There were no public comments during the public hearing.

On a motion by Councilmember Bennett, seconded by Councilmember Garrett, Council
voted to close the public hearing.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

Scheduled Public Appearances – None

Mayor’s Comments
Mayor Harvey thanked Mr. Gillespie and Mr. Elder for all their efforts in prepping the
area at the Public Works Building Site.  Their efforts along with the Public Works
Department have saved the Town a tremendous amount of money.

Consent Agenda
On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember McDearmon,
Council voted to approve the consent agenda as presented for May 14, 2012.  All
members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

Unfinished Business
None

On a motion by Councilmember Garrett, seconded by Councilmember Spiggle, Council
voted to amend the Accounts Receivable Water & Sewer policy #7 - $25 disconnect fee
will charged for any customer cut-off for non-payment and a $25 reconnect fee will be
charged to restore service.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember McDearmon,
Council voted to appoint Allen Austin to the Appomattox Town Tree Board with a term
expiring December 31, 2013.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

None

None

On a motion by Councilmember McDearmon, seconded by Councilmember Garrett,
Council voted to adjourn the meeting at 7:50 p.m.  All members present voting aye.
Motion carried.
