content: On a motion by S. E. Carter, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to allow Mr. Britton to be Project Manager for the Town of Pamplin. All those present voting aye.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council accepted the recommendations of the Water Committee with the correction that the attendant be on duty from 6:00 P.M. to 11:00 P.M. and Council would give Mr. Wilson until April 15, 1985 to get asphalt on the driveway.

Carter-aye Conner-aye Bass-aye Webb-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 10:15 P.M.

R. C. Spiggle, MAYOR

Bobbie H. Mullins, CLERK
