date: 1984-01-01
content: On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, and all those present voting aye, Town Council agreed to grant the extended leave of absence to Robert L. Miller, until the first of January, 1985. Motion carried.

Mr. Hubbard made the motion, seconded by Mr. Bass, to adopt the 1985 Budget as presented with the recommendation that a ceiling amount be placed on the Blue Cross Blue Shield Insurance of $2400.00 and all over would be the employees responsibility.

Hubbard-aye Bass-aye Conner-aye Webb-aye Noble-aye Carter-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council set the 1985 Tax Rate at $.20 per $100.00 for Real Estate and $.55 per $100.00 for Personal Property.

Hubbard-aye Bass-aye Webb-aye Carter-aye Noble-aye Conner-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Town Council voted to raise the Auto Tag to $15.00 each as of January 1, 1985.

Hubbard-aye Conner-aye Webb-aye Noble-aye Carter-aye Bass-aye Motion carried.

Mr. Hubbard made the motion, seconded by Mr. Bass, to set the salaries and that all other rates remain the same for 1985.

Carter-aye Conner-aye Webb-aye Noble-aye Bass-aye Hubbard-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted to appropriate the funds as follows for the 1985 Budget:

GENERAL FUND EXPENDITURES 297,215.00
GENERAL FUND RECEIPTS 297,215.00

WATER FUND EXPENDITURES 92,200.00
WATER FUND RECEIPTS 92,200.00

SEWER FUND EXPENDITURES 46,300.00
SEWER FUND RECEIPTS 46,300.00

SEWER FUND MANDATED PROJECTS 1,080,000.00
SEWER FUND E.P.A. GRANT 1,080,000.00

REVENUE SHARING EXPENDITURES 18,500.00
REVENUE SHARING RECEIPTS 18,500.00

Conner-aye Carter-aye Bass-aye Webb-aye Noble-aye Hubbard-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted to table Item 5, Old Business, approval of amendments of 1984 Budget and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.
