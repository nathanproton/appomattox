date: 1986-06-01
content: On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to re-appoint Mr. Hubbard and Mr. Waddell to the Appomattox Planning Commission to represent the Town for a term of office thru June 1986.

Carter-aye Conner-aye Noble-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted to adopt the Litter Resolution in conjunction with the County of Appomattox and the Town of Pamplin. (See attached copy)

Carter-aye Conner-aye Noble-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to authorize the Town Manager to handle the situation with Harold Nash's traffic fine, and all members present voting aye.
Motion carried unanimously.

A request from Steve Pool for a Street Light on his property on Route 460 was referred to the Street Committee for their study and recommendations.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 8:32 P.M.
