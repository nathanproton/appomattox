content: Mr. Britton was requested to check on cleaning up the property on Route 460 at Jack Lawson's property at the sidewalk.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel and Property Acquisition and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

Council reconvened into regular session.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Nobbe, Council accepted the raise from the County of Appomattox for Mr. Britton and to make this retroactive to July 1, 1984. (See copy attached)

Conner-aye Bass-aye Webb-aye Hubbard-aye Nobbe-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Nobbe, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council accepted the D. S. Nash's proposal for waterline loop on Route 24, contingent upon the National Park Service providing the materials for the project.

Conner-aye Bass-aye Nobbe-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Nobbe, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council agreed to the National Park Service's project as proposed concerning the waterline loop on Route 24, contingent upon D. S. Nash's project.

Conner-aye Bass-aye Nobbe-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 8:50 P.M.
