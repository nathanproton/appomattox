On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted not to change the Town Code regarding Game Rooms, as per Mr. Cox's request and all members present voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel, and all members present voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

Council reconvened into Regular Session at 9:10 P.M., on a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Noble, and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council approved the hiring of Peggy W. Rogers, at $4.00 per hour, for a maximum of seven weeks, during Mrs. Mullins medical leave, and all members present voting aye.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to pay Judy Volturo for all over the regular 35 hours, she works at regular time (her hourly rate of pay).

It was general consensus of Town Council that a "ceiling" be placed on the person hired to become the "Working Foreman" at $18,000.00. The "Salary" when the person is hired will be brought back to Council.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to expend the additional wages authorized for Ms. Rogers and Ms. Volturo out of savings, General Fund, and all members present voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:35 P.M.
