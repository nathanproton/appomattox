The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on July 16, 1956, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor Richard D. Robinson presiding.

The following members were present: T. Hunter Dickerson, G. E. Smith, J. C. Caldwell, Mrs. C. H. Robinson and J. T. Irby.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved as read.

Mr. W. W. Scott met with the Town Council to explain the findings of Isbell Street in regard to a side walk.

A motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson that the Town engage Mr. W. W. Scott to make a survey of the possibility of putting in a sewer plant for the Fleshman subdivision. Motion carried.

A Motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by T. Hunter Dickerson that the Property Committee meet with the Board of Supervisors to see about obtaining another site for a community dump. Motion carried.

A Motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson that the Town give the Teen Age Club the minimum amount of water each month. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of August 20, 1956.
