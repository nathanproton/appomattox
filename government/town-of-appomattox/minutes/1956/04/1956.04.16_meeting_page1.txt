content: APRIL MEETING
The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office, on April 16, 1956, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor Richard D. Robinson presiding. The following members were present: G. E. Smith, J. T. Irby, T. Hunter Dickerson, J. C. Caldwell, and Mrs. C. H. Robinson.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved as read.

A Motion was made by J. C. Caldwell, seconded by J. T. Irby, that the Town furnish Jennings Conner water if he will run a line to the end of the present water line. Motion carried.

A motion was made by J. T. Irby, seconded by G. E. Smith that the Town Council request the Virginia Department of Highways to return to the Town of Appomattox four (4) feet for a side walk on the south side of Isbell Street (state Secondary Road 1003) from State Route 131 to Route 460. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of May 21, 1956.
