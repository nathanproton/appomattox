The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on June 16, 1956, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor Richard D. Robinson presiding. The following members were present: J. T. Irby, T. Hunter Dickerson, C. L. Smith, W. S. Gillis, J. C. Caldwell, and Mrs. C. H. Robinson.
The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved as read.
A Motion was made by J. T. Irby, seconded by W. S. Gillis, that the sewer line be extended to Charlie Palmer's place. Motion carried.
Motion was made by J. C. Caldwell, seconded by C. L. Smith that the street in front of Carver High School from Rt. 460 west to Court Street be named Cawthorne Avenue. Motion carried.
A motion was made by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, seconded by T. Hunter Dickerson that the remainder of Lee Crawl Avenue west to Church Street and part of Cawthorne Avenue be taken over by the Highway Department for maintenance, Motion carried.
