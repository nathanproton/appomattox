content: A special meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held in the Council Room on December 27, 1967 at 7:00 p.m. with Mayor H. R. Pace presiding.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

Members present were: Mrs. Calvin Robinson, Burke Guill, R. C. Hubbard, Ivey Holland, and Martin Lipford.

The proposed budget for 1968 was discussed and adopted.

The meeting was adjourned.

Signed H. R. Pace Mayor
Signed C. S. Booth Clerk
