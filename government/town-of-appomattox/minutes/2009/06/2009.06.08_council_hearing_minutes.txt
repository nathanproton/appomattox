1  Public Hearing

Monday, June 8, 2009

The Appomattox Town Council held a public hearing on Monday, June 8, 2009 at 7:00 pm at the
Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia with Mayor Harvey
presiding.

Members present:  Bryan J. Baine, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, C. Lewis McDearmon, John T.
“Plicky” Williams, Karl E. Carter, Jennifer Jamerson-Scruggs.

Others:  Carlton Duck, Derek Norfield, Barbara Shelton, William Goodrich, June Goodrich,
Mary Lou Spiggle, Timmy Garrett, Jonathan Garrett, Bart S. Van Nieuwenhuise, Town Manager
and Roxanne Paulette, Town Clerk.

Citizen Comments:
Barbara Shelton – There is an awful lot in the tourism budget.
Mr. Van Nieuwenhuise – It is about the same as last year.
Barbara Shelton – Still too much
Mr. Van Nieuwenhuise – So noted.
Barbara Shelton – Was the $8000 for fireworks in the budget?
Mr. Van Nieuwenhuise – It will be taken from the FY 09 tourism budget

Mr. Goodrich – If the town connects to the waterline what effect will it have on the water rates?
Mr. Van Nieuwenhuise – If the town were to connect to the water line it would not be within the
next budget year therefore having no effect on the rates for the FY 2010.
