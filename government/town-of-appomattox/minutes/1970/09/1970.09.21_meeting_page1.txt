content: The regular meeting of the Town Council was held in the Council Room on September 21, 1970 at 8:00 P.M. with Mayor H. B. Pack presiding.

Members present were Mrs. Calvin Robinson, Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, and R. C. Hubbard.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved.

The mayor reported that James R. Ford, Jr. has been appointed as full time police officer.

The Mayor announced that future Council meetings will start at 7:30 P.M.

Hubbard moved and Guill seconded that Thomas E. Conner be elected to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of L. I. Bass. Motion carried unanimously.

The Mayor announced that the Public Hearing was held on September 19, 1970, but that the Planning Commission has not met yet to make recommendations on the proposed Zoning Ordinance revisions.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed H. B. Pack Mayor

Signed C. S. Booth Clerk
