content: A regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held in the Council Room on January 19, 1970 at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor H. B. Pack, presiding.

Members present were Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, R. C. Hubbard, and M. L. Webb.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

There was no old or new business.

Mayor Pack told the members that an Industrial Committee was being set up for the Town & County for the purpose of getting new industries in.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed H. B. Pack Mayor

Signed C. S. Booth Clerk
