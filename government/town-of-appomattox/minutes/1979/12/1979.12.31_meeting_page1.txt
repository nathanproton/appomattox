content: RECONVENED MEETING - DECEMBER 31, 1979
The recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held at 10:00 A.M., Monday, December 31, 1979, in the Town Office, with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those present were: T. W. Lawson, R. L. Bass, R. C. Hubbard, and M. E. Gordon.
Absent: T. E. Conner and S. E. Carter.

Also present were Beck Hudson, Reporter, Clerk B. H. Mullins, and Town Manager Britton.

Mr. Bass made the motion, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, to adopt the 1980 Budget as presented.

Bass-aye Gordon-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Gordon, Council voted to authorize Ronald Spiggle to sign and execute the VEPCO agreement.

Bass-aye Gordon-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before the Council, the meeting adjourned at 10:30 P.M.
