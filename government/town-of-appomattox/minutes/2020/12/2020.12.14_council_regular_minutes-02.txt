1  Appomattox Town Council
Regular Council Meeting
December 14, 2020

The Appomattox Town Council held a Regular Council meeting on Monday, December 14, 2020
at 7:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia
with Mayor Richard Conner, presiding.

Council members present – James J. Boyce, Aaron Tilton, Nathan A. Simpson, Mary Lou
Spiggle, Timothy W. Garrett, and Claudia G. Puckette (via phone).

Staff members present – Gary Shanaberger, Town Manager; Kim Ray, Treasurer; and Roxanne
W. Casto, Clerk of Council; and Jeff Elder, Facilities Director.

Others present – Susan Chapman, Brown, Edwards & Company, Carlton Duck, Alive Media,
Michelle Murch, Times Virginian newspaper and Christian Raymond.

The meeting was also broadcast Live on the Town of Appomattox Facebook page and public
phone participation through FreeConferenceCall.com was available.

Mayor Conner called the meeting to order.

Rev. Carlton Duck, Pastor of the Gethsemane Baptist Church led the prayer and the Pledge of
Allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America.

Mayor’s Comments:
May the blessings of a safe holiday season be upon all of you.

Scheduled Public Appearances:
Susan Chapman, CPA with Brown Edwards & Company, LLP presented the fiscal year 2020
Annual Audit report for the Town of Appomattox and answered questions of the Town Council.

Adoption of the Consent Agenda:
On a motion by Mr. T. Garrett, seconded by Ms. Spiggle, Council voted to approve the consent
agenda for December 14, 2020 with the addition of New Business #3 – Consideration of bonuses
for Town funded deputies of the Appomattox County Sheriff’s Office.  All members present
voting aye.  Motion carried 6-0.

Unfinished Business:
None

New Business:
On a motion by Mr. Simpson, seconded by Mr. Boyce, Council voted to accept the FY 2020
Annual Audit for the Town of Appomattox from Brown, Edwards & Company, LLP.  All
members present voting aye.  Motion carried 6-0.

On a motion by Mr. Simpson, seconded by Mr. Boyce, Council voted to adopt the following
budget amendment resolutions:

All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Boyce, seconded by Mr. Tilton, Council voted to fund a request of $10,000
plus FICA for the two Town assigned deputies of the Appomattox County Sheriff’s Office.  Roll
call vote:  Tilton – yes, Spiggle – yes, Boyce – yes, Garrett – yes, Puckette – yes, Simpson –
abstained.  Motion carried 5-0.

Council Standing Committee Reports:
None

Citizens Comment Period:
Christian Raymond, P O Box 2882, Appomattox, Virginia appeared before Council as Chairman
of the Appomattox Republican Committee to state the position the committee is taking to the
recent news of former Governor McAuliffe’s announcement to run for Governor of Virginia in
2021.

Town Manager’s Report:
Mr. Shanaberger provided the following updates:

➢  The Request for Proposals for a Financial Analysis will begin soon.
➢  Church Street Waterline – The Town continues to work with Hurt & Proffitt to answer

questions from Rural Development.

➢  Appomattox Downtown Revitalization Project:  3 more properties are under contract and

is expecting 3 more this week.

➢  Phase II includes 11 properties and bids are due December 22, 2020.
➢  A lot of construction should start in late January, early February 2021.
➢  Thanked staff and Council & Merry Christmas.

Council Comments:
Mrs. Puckette – Thanked Kim Ray for completing a nearly impossible task.

Mr. Simpson – Wished a Happy Hanukkah, Happy Kwanzaa and Merry Christmas to everyone.
