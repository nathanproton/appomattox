6:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia with
Mayor Conner, presiding.

Town Council present:  Aaron Tilton, Nathan A. Simpson, Timothy W. Garrett, Mary Lou

Mayor Conner called to order the Council Workshop meeting.

Greater Meadowlark Community Development Block Grant Update:
Matt Perkins, Central Virginia Planning District Commission provided an update on the Greater
Meadowlark Housing Grant project.  The Town of Appomattox was awarded a $1,000,000
multi-year grant, $700,000 1st phase (housing rehabilitation) and $300,000 2nd phase
(infrastructure improvements).  The goals of the project are to create safe, sanitary, and secure
conditions.  There were housing rehabilitations completed on 10 owner occupied dwellings and 5
investor owned dwellings.  Easements were secured for 10 streetlights along Patterson Street.
There were two (2) structures removed due to blight.  A total of 89 individuals were assisted by
this project.  Several stormwater drainage issues were addressed along with the drainage ditch
expansion on Patterson Street.

Council thanked Mr. Perkins for providing the update.

Closed Session:
On a motion by Mr. Garrett, seconded by Ms. Spiggle, Council voted to convene in Closed
Session pursuant to §2.2-3711 (A) 1 of the Code of Virginia, 1950, as amended, for the purpose
of discussion of prospective candidates for appointment for the council vacancy created by the
death of Councilman Paul D. Harvey on October 19, 2020 and pursuant to Section 2.2-3711 (A)
8 of the Code of Virginia, 1950, as amended, for the purpose of consultation with legal counsel
retained by the Town regarding specific legal matters requiring the provision of legal advice by
such counsel, specifically being legal issues related to the Appomattox Christmas Parade
application issued by the Virginia Department of Transportation as it relates to the Governor’s
Covid-19 orders.
Vote:  Tilton – yes, Spiggle – yes, Garrett – yes, Simpson – yes, Puckette – yes.  Motion carried
5-0.
