January 11, 1988

It is the general consensus of Town Council to write letters to the
businesses downtown about the increased problem about parking on
Main Street and if this doesn't work, then we have a Town Police
Officer enforce the 2 hour parking limit for Main Street.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted
to adopt the following resolution:

Resolved, by the Appomattox Town Council at
its regular scheduled meeting on January 11,
1988, that effective January 1, 1988,

the following policy will be in effect:

1. All Town Employees will when either
operating or riding in a Town owned
vehicle will wear his/her seat belt
at all times,

2. There will be no unauthorized persons
riding in or operating a Town owned.
vehicle. An authorized person is
hereby defined as a person on official
Town Business...

Bass~-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye Mitchell-aye
Webb-aye Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.
