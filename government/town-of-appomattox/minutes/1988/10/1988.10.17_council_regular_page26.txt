26. Recessed and Reconvened Meeting - October 17, 1988

Thée=recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town
Council was held on Monday, October 17, 1988, at 7:00 P.M.
with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those present were: R. L. Bass, H. W. Conner, W. H. Carson,
Marvin Mitchell, and S. E. Carter. Absent - R. C. Hubbard

Others present: Bernard C. Proctor, P.E. and Bobbie
H. Mullins, Clerk.

The purpose of the meeting was to go over the Sewer Use
Ordinance as it relates to the Town Code.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned. ,

C, “MAYOR
R. C. Spiggle

Bobbie H.
