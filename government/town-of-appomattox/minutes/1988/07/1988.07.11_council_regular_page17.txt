Regular Meeting - July 11, 1988 be.

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held
on Monday, July 11, 1988, at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor Spiggle
presiding.

Those members present: W. H. Carson, R. L. Bass, Marvin Mitchell,
S. E. Carter, and H. W. Conner. Absent - R. C. Hubbard.

Others present: H. T. Nash, Sr., Hugh Radcliffe, and
Bobbie Mullins, Clerk.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council adopted
the Agenda with one addition of New Business: 2. Water Availability
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

Harold Nash, Superintendent of Public Works, appeared before
Council to discuss the water availability in the Town of Appomattox.
Mr. Nash and the Mayor will continue to work on this situation.

Mr. Hugh Radcliffe, representing skateboarders, appeared before
Council regarding the skateboarding on Town Streets. @G@ouncil was
of the opinion that the problem seems to have quieted down now
and they will not enact an ordinance at this time.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council approved
the Council minutes of the June 13, 1988, meeting and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle presented the letter as drawn up by Bernard C.
Proctor, P.E., to notify the citizens of the proposed sewer rate
and why we have to raise rates.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted
to go into Executive Session in accordance with Section 2.1-344,
of the State Code to discuss Personnel, and all those members
present voting aye. Motion carried.
