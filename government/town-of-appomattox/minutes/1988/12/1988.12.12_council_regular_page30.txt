December 12, 1988

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. S. E. Carter,
Council voted to adopt a fee of Water - $500.00 connection
fee and $500.00 availability fee and Sewer $500.00 connection
fee and $500.00 availability fee.

Bass-aye Carson-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye
Mitchell-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council
approved the Financial Statement and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council

voted in accordance with Section 2.1-344 of the Town Code,
an Executive Session be held to discuss Personnel, and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On.a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to reconvene into regular session and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

Council voted to table Item 4, of the Agenda, on a motion
by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to advertize the proposed 1989 Budget and hold a
Public Hearing on Thursday, December 29, 1988 at the Town
Office at 5:00 P.M. and all those present voting aye.

Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to recess and reconvene on Thursday, December 29, 1988,
at 5:00 P.M. and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.
