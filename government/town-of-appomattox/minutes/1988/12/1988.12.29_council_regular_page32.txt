Recessed and Reconvened Meeting - December 29, 1988

The recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town
Council was held on Thursday, December 29, 1988, at 5:00
P.M. with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those present were: R. C. Hubbard, W. H. Carson,
R. L. Bass, Marvin Mitchell, S. E. Carter, and H. W. Conner.

Others present: Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.

Mayor Spiggle reported on the proposal from Alexander &
Alexander on Workmen's Compensation.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to accept the proposal on Self-Insured Program for
Workmen's Compensation.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Hubbard-aye Conner-aye Mitchell-aye
Carson-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to adopt the 1989 Budget for the Town of Appomattox.
