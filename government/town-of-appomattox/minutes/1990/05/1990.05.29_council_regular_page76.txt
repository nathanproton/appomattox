Recessed and Reconvened - May 29, 1990

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to deny the request of Mr. Jennings for a Conditional
Use Permit and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to grant Mr. Houchens request to abandon Lizzie
Street provided that he pays all cost of closing the street,
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned.
