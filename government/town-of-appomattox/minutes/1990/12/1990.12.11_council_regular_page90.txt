Council minutes - December 11, 1990

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted
to accept the request from David Hannan, Shoppes at Appomattox,
in regard to moving the lights on the Shopping Center right of
way and contact Virginia Power so that this can be done, and
also, the cost of these lights in the future will be borne by
the Town.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Carson-aye Conner-aye Mitchell-aye
Hubbard-aye Motion carried.

The Council agreed to let the employees have off for
Christmas all day December 24 and December 25th. The office
will be open all day December 31st.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to advertize their intent to adopt the proposed 1991
Budget and hold a public hearing on December 19, 1990, at
4:30 P.M. , and ali those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard,’ seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to recess and reconvene on December 31, 1990 at

4:30 P.M. to adopt the Budget for 1991 and alli those present
voting aye. Motion carried.
