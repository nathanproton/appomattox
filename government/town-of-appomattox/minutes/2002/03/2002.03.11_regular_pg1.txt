# REGULAR MEETING – March 11, 2002

Page 1 of 4


' The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on Monday, March 11,

2002, at 7:30 PM with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present were: Steve Lawson, Joyce Bennett, C. Lewis McDearmon,
Norman Mayberry, Marvin Mitchell, and W. H. Carson.

Others present: Wayne Phelps, Lee & Charlotte Munsick, Lewis Edward Moore, Will
Pettus, Tamara Jamerson, John & Patsy Wilson, Mitch Arrington, Shanna Webber,
Sammy Webber, Samuel Webber,Jr., Maryann Mostrangel, Bryan Mostrangel, Majorie
Goetz, Betty Roberts, William Phillips, Town Attorney, Lisa Knott, Times Virginian,
Tom & Kathy Bloodworth, David T. Gairett, Jr., Town Manager, and Bobbie H. Mullins,
Clerk. :

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council voted to approve the

Consent Agenda as presented, and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

Lewis Edward Moore spoke in favor of the Volunteers for Tourism and asked that we
financially support their endeavors.

Lee Munsick — Asked for the support of the Volunteer Committee working on Tourism in
the Downtown Area. :

Mary Mostrangel spoke for Linda & Bobby Shupe of B & L Sales regarding their support
to the Volunteers on Downtown Tourism. They asked that we give the $1,000.00 to aid
them in their pursuit of the Tourism.

John L. Wilson appeared before Council to request three items. He wants the Town to
close the streets on several occasions during the year to have the downtown celebrations.
He would also like to see the Town have a Downtown Market. Third, he asked that once
again, we consider donating $1,000.00 to print the brochures that has been developed by
the Volunteer Committee.

Mr. Will Pettus, FFA, spoke to Council regarding their proposal to repair and repaint at
the Town Kiddie Park as a Community Project. Council agreed by consensus that they
move forward with their plans. :
