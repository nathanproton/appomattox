A meeting of the Appomattox Town Council and Appomattox County Board of .
Supervisors with the Appomattox Planning Commission was held on Monday, July 29,
2002 at 7:00 p.m. at the Courthouse.

The meeting was called to order by Mayor Spiggle.

Members present: S. E. Lawson, Joyce Bennett, Marvin Mitchell and Lewis
McDearmon.
