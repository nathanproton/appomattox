_. WORKSHOP. MEETING = October.29,.2002.. = sc. 20. =

A workshop meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on October 29, 2002 at
7:00 pan. at the Appomattox Town Office with Mayor Spiggle, presiding.

Members present: W. H. Carson, N. H. Mayberry, Joyce Bennett, C. L, McDearmon,

S. E. Lawson, Marvin Mitchell.

Others present: Jackie Mann, Nita Mann, Josh Rubinstein, VRWA, Eric Shortt, VRWA,
Ted Dean, ATS International, David Garrett, Larry Mobley, Bill Phillips and Roxanne
Paulette, Deputy Clerk. ‘

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell to approve the petition as
presented by Jackie & Nita Mann with the conditional use that the game room be
supervised by an adult during its hours of operation. All voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Mayberry that the approval applies only
to the petitioners, Jackie & Nita Mann. All voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion made by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell to approve the Joint Water
Study RFP with Appomattox County. All voting aye. . Motion carried. ‘

On a motion made by Mr. McDearmon, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, to approve the Well
Option Agreement with the Appomattox County School Board at the new Elementary
School, All voting aye. Motion carried.

Eric Shortt & Josh Rubinstein along with Ted Dean gave a short presentation to Town
Council conceming fractured rock and how water reaches the water table. Mr. Dean also

gave some scenarios of how to find water.

With no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 9:00 p.m.
