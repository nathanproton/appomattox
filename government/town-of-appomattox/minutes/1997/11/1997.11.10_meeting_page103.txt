Council Minutes - November 10, 1997

On a motion by Mr. S. E. Carter, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council voted to approve

the Holiday Schedule which consists of Wednesday one half day off on November 26th,

on Thanksgiving, Christmas on Wednesday, December 24th one half day off, and Friday,
December 26th off, and Friday, January 2nd for New Years, and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted to transfer funds
in the amount of $10,000.00 from Water System Improvements to Sewer Capital
Outlay.

Lawson-aye Mitchell-aye Pack-aye Carter-aye

Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Carter, the following resolutions be
endorsed and approved. (See enclosed copy)

Carter-aye Lawson-aye Mitchell-aye Pack-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to recess and
reconvene on Thursday, November 13, 1997 at 7:00 PM to complete the Agenda,
and all those present voting aye.
