1  Appomattox Town Council
Regular Council Meeting
July 13, 2015

The Appomattox Town Council held a Regular Council meeting on Monday, July 13, 2015
at 7:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia
with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present:  Timothy W. Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr., Mary Lou Spiggle, M. Erin
Finch and Claudia G. Puckette.

Absent:  Steven T. Conner

Others present:  Daniel Puckette, Times Virginian; Carlton Duck and Brian Moore, Alive Media
TV; Nathan Harbin and Clara Aleman, HDLA; Rev. Chris Brooks, Elizabeth Milton, Kristen
Harbin, Noah Harris, Jeff Elder, Facilities Director; Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer; and
Roxanne Casto, Town Clerk.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order.

Rev. Chris Brooks, Glory Baptist Church opened the meeting with a prayer and led the Pledge of
Allegiance.

Public Hearing:
07132015-01 – Appomattox Town Code Chapter 185-16
Mayor Harvey opened the public hearing to receive public comments concerning adoption of
amended and reenacted Town Code Section 185-16 – Failure to pay license fee or personal
property tax; registration withholding; violations and penalties.

The amendment is as follows:
It shall be unlawful for any owner of a motor vehicle to fail to pay the local license fee or
personal property taxes assessed with respect to each motor vehicle after September 30 of each
year, and with respect to any fees not paid at that time, a fee of $40 to reimburse the Town of
Appomattox for costs associated with administration of the Vehicle Registration Withholding
Program with the Commonwealth of Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles shall be assessed.

There were no public comments.

Mayor Harvey closed the public hearing.

Public Hearing:
07132015-02 – USDA Rural Development
Mayor Harvey opened the public hearing to comments regarding the Town’s intent to apply for
USDA Rural Development funds to assist in the replacement of the town’s 60+ year old
Trickling Filter Facility.  Comments regarding economic and environmental impacts, service
area, alternatives to the project, and other related project issues are encouraged.

There were no public comments.

Mayor Harvey closed the public hearing.

Mayor’s Comments:
Mayor Harvey welcomed Ms. Erin Finch to Council.  Ms. Finch was chosen to fill the unexpired
term of the late Mr. Mayberry.

Mayor Harvey informed Council they would be receiving an update on upgrades to the Kiddie
Park.  Jeff Elder, Roxanne Casto and Mayor Harvey met with HDLA the week prior to finalize
the concepts in the presentation.

Scheduled Public Appearances –
Harvey Design Land Architects – Nathan Harbin and Clara Aleman presented a PowerPoint
presentation of the Preliminary Design for the upgrades at the Kiddie Park.

Mrs. Puckette asked that the Town make the park as safe as possible.

Ms. Spiggle added that the kids will love climbing and sitting on all of it.  She agrees with the
lime color selection.

The recommendation from staff is to ask Council to do a bid package including bathrooms.  The
Town would have an add-on of the security cameras.  The Town could possibly receive the
security cameras as a donation.

Construction drawings and the identification of equipment should be completed by August 1,
2015.  The project will then be out to bid for 30 days, with bids due in September 2015.

Consent Agenda –
On a motion by Mr. McDearmon, seconded by Ms. Spiggle, Council voted to adopt the consent
agenda with the addition of New Business #4 – Consideration to accept the Finance & Planning
Committee recommendation to accept the FY 2014 Annual Audit from Brown, Edwards &
Company, LLP and New Business #5 – Consideration to advertise the Kiddie Park upgrades.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

Unfinished Business – none

New Business –

Mayor Harvey opened the floor for nominations of Vice-Mayor.  Mr. McDearmon nominated
Mr. Garrett.  There were no other nominations.  Mayor Harvey closed the floor.

On a motion by Ms. Spiggle, seconded by Mr. McDearmon, Council voted to elect Mr. Timothy
W. “Timmy” Garrett as Vice Mayor until the Council term ending June 30, 2015.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

On a motion by Ms. Spiggle, seconded by Ms. Puckette, Council voted to write off uncollected
debts, as presented by Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer, that have exceeded the statute of
limitations.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

On a motion by Mr. McDearmon, seconded by Mrs. Puckette, Council voted to amend and
reenact the Appomattox Town Code – Chapter 185, Section 16 – Failure to pay license fee or
personal property tax; registration withholding; violations and penalties.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

On a recommendation by Mr. McDearmon, Chairman of the Finance & Planning Committee,
Council voted to accept the FY 2014 Annual Audit Report from Brown, Edwards & Company,
LLC.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

On a motion by Mrs. Puckette, seconded by Mr. Garrett, Council voted to authorize HDLA to
create the bid package and advertise the Kiddie Park project.
Vote:  Garrett – yes, McDearmon – yes, Spiggle – yes, Finch – yes, Puckette – yes.
Absent:  Conner
Motion carried 5-0.

Town Manager’s Report – Mr. Gillespie is on vacation.

Citizen Comment Period – none

Council Concerns – none
