Regular meeting-April 14, 1980

WHEREAS, Linden Street from the existing State maintained, east for a distance of 130 feet, has a right-of-way of 30 feet, as required by the State Highway Department; and

WHEREAS, the Town of Appomattox guarantees the right-of-way exist and is the required unencroached 30 foot, as shown on the Zoning Map, of the Town of Appomattox, drawn by May-Hines & Associates, Consulting Engineers; and

WHEREAS, Atwood Street from the intersection of Lee Street, for a distance of 440 feet to the intersection of Highland Avenue has a right-of-way of 30 feet as required by the State Highway Department; and

WHEREAS, the Town of Appomattox guarantees the right-of-way exists and is the required unencroached 30 foot as shown on the Zoning Map of the Town of Appomattox, drawn by May-Hines & Asso., Consulting Engineers; and

WHEREAS, East Harrell Street from the State Maintenance sign for a distance of 350 feet has a right-of-way of 30 foot as required by the State Department of Highways; and

WHEREAS, the Town of Appomattox guarantees the right-of-way exists and is the required unencroached 30 foot as shown on the Zoning Map of the Town of Appomattox, drawn by May-Hines & Asso. Consulting Engineers; and

WHEREAS, Price Lane from the State Maintenance sign for a distance of 100 feet has a right-of-way of 30 foot and as required by the Highway Department; and

WHEREAS, the Town of Appomattox guarantees the right-of-way exists and is the required unencroached 30 foot as shown on the Zoning Map of the Town of Appomattox, drawn by May-Hines & Assoc. Consulting Engineers; and

WHEREAS, Ethel Street for a distance of 400 feet has a right-of-way of 30 feet as required by State Department of Highways; and

WHEREAS, the Town of Appomattox guarantees the right-of-way exists and is the required unencroached 30 foot as shown on the Zoning Map, of the Town of Appomattox, drawn by May-Hines & Assoc. Consulting Engineers; and

WHEREAS, all of these streets as mentioned above had 30 foot right-of-way's prior to the adoption of the Appomattox Zoning Ordinance of 1969;

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, the Town of Appomattox requests the Virginia Department of Highways to take over for maintenance and/or construction Linden Street, as stated above, Atwood Street, as stated above, East Harrell Street, as stated above, Price Lane, as stated above, and Ethel Street, as stated above, for the 1979-80 allocation for the Virginia Department of Highways.

Conner-aye Carter-aye Bass-aye Gordon-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried unanimously.
