Carson-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council adopted the proposed 1981 Budget as presented by the Finance Committee. (Copy filed in Council file dated December 1980.)

Carson-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

Mr. Jack Torbett, representing the Boxing Club and Phillip Seay, requested permission to use the Depot for their Boxing Club which is sponsored by the Fraternal Order of the Police.

After a brief discussion by Council, Mr. Carson made the motion, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted to deny the request from Phillip Seay for use of the Depot and all members voted aye.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:00.
