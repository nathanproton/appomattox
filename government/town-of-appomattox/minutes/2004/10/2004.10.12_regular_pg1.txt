REGULAR MEETING - October 12, 2004

A regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held.on Tuesday, October 12,
2004 at 7:30 p.m. at the Municipal Building with Mayor Spiggle, presiding.

Members present: J oyce Bennett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr., J ohn 1. Wilson, Steve E.
Lawson. Absent: N. H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Marvin H. Mitchell.

Others present: Lillie Adams, Herb Chambers, Nelson Pankey, Carlton Duck, Chad
Denton, Times Virginian Newspaper, Larry Mobley, Frank A. Wright, Jr., Town
Attorney, David T. Garrett, Jr., Town Manager, Roxanne W. Mundy, Town Clerk.

Mayor Spiggle welcomed all the visitors,
Mayor Spiggle opened the meeting with an invocation.

On a motion by Councilmember Bennett, seconded by Councilmember McDearmon,
Council voted to approve the consent agenda with the addition of NB-4 Closed Session.
All members present, voting aye. Motion carried.

There were no public comments.

Mr. Herb Chambers, Representative of the Appomattox Lion’s Club appeared before
Council to discuss safety issues at the Shopping Center on Route 460.

Standing Committee Reports —

Property & Street Committee —
On a recommendation by Councilmember Lawson, the Street & Property Committee
recommended the purchase of 12 additional banners with brackets to be erected on street
lamp poles for Church Street and Court Street adjacent to Main Street, in addition to
those already purchased for Main Street. All members present voting aye. Motion
carried,

Councilmember Bennett commended the Town Manager and Town Crew for work
performed during the Railroad Festival. Everything looked very nice.

David Garrett asked for clear direction on the Lee Grant Avenue sidewalk project.
VDOT has questioned the brick edging for the sidewalk. Council reiterated to the Town
Manager that it does want to include the brick edging on all sidewalk-projects, inchiding
Lee Grant Avenue. .
