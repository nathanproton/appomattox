content: APRIL MEETING

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on April 19, 1965, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson, presiding.

The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, T. R. Gilbert, Burke Guill, Dick Wingfield and Ivey Holland.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion was made by Dick Wingfield, seconded by Burke Guill, That the Town purchase a half ad in the Appomattox Junior Chamber of Commerce Publication. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of May 17, 1965.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor

Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk

MAY MEETING

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on May 17, 1965, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson, presiding.

The following members were present: Dick Wingfield; T. R. Gilbert; Mrs. C. H. Robinson; and H. B. Pack.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion was made by Dick Wingfield, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, That the Town renew the Lease on the Appomattox Ball Park to the Appomattox Ball Park Improvement Association for a period of three years beginning May 18, 1965. Motion carried.

Motion made by H. B. Pack, seconded by Burke Guill, That the Town request the Highway Department to take over for Improvement and Maintenance that portion of Annie Street, which is 0.13 of a mile, for the rest of the Town quota for 1965 ending June 30, 1965. Motion carried.

Motion was made by T. R. Gilbert, seconded by Dick Wingfield, That Snead's Pharmacy be charged the full out-of-town water rates from January 1, 1965. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of June 21, 1965.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor

Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk
