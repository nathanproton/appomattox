content: The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on March 29, 1965, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson presiding.

The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson; Burke Guill; Ivey Holland; H. B. Pack; T. R. Gilbert and Dick Wingfield.

Minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion was made by H. B. Pack, seconded by Ivey Holland, that the town open up and gravel Fleshman Street. Motion carried.

Motion made by H. B. Pack and seconded by Burke Guill, that 'No Parking' signs be put up from the Holiness Church to 460 on the right side next to the school. Motion carried.

Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Dick Wingfield, that the Town pay S. R. Lawson $16.00 for the flag to be put up at the Town Office. Motion carried.

Motion was made by H. B. Pack, seconded by T. R. Gilbert, that the Town make a donation to the Rescue Squad for the amount of the water bill at the end of the year. Motion carried.

A motion was made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Burke Guill, that the Town purchase the chlorinator for the sewer plant and other necessary equipment. Motion carried.

There being no other business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of April 19, 1965.
