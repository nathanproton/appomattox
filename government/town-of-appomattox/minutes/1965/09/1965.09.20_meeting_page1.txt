content: The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on September 20, 1965 at 7:30 p.m. with Mayor H.B. Peck presiding. The following members were present: R.L. Bass, Mrs. C.H. Robinson, Burke Guill, and Ivey Holland. Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Burke Guill, that Mrs. Robinson is elected Vice-Chairman of the Town Council. Motion carried. The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read. Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Dick Bass, that the Town will cooperate with the School Board in providing the services to the best of our ability. Motion carried. Motion made by Dick Bass, seconded by Ivey Holland, that the Town Council of the Town of Appomattox that the Town of Appomattox will not be responsible for cleaning or doing anything to septic tanks in the Town of Appomattox until authorized by the Town Manager.
