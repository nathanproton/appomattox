Dear Mrs. Gilbert:

Enclosed is a copy of the resolution prepared in tribute to Mr. Gilbert's many contributions to our community, and especially to honor his memory as a valuable member of the Appomattox Town Council.

We make this presentation in gratitude for Mr. Gilbert's service and friendship with all of us. All of the members of Council, Mayor H. B. Pugh and Town Manager Charles J. Hunter extend to you and all of Mr. Gilbert's family our deepest sympathy.

With kindest regards,

Mrs. Calvin Robinson
President,
Appomattox Town Council
