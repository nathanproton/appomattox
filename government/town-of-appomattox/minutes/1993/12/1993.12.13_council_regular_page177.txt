Council Minutes
December 13, 1993

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to appoint S. E. Lawson to the Low Interest Housing
Mortgage Fund Board for Central Virginia Planning District
Commission and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

In accordance with Section 2.1-344 of the State Code, and on

a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted

to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel and

Personnel Ofily. All members present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted
to go back into Regular Session and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried. Bobbie Mullins, Clerk, so noted that only
Personnel items were discussed.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted
to increase the raise for Billy Franklin to 5% for 1994 and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to advertize our intent to adopt the Proposed 1994
Budget and hold a Public Hearing on Wednesday, December 29,
1993 at 5:00 PM, and all those present voting aye. Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to Recess and Reconvene on December 29, 1993, at 5:00
PM. to adopt the 1994 Budget and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle announced a Workshop for January 4th on the
Town Code.

There being no further business to come before the Council,

the meeting adjourned.
