Council Meeting - January 11, 1993

Mayor Spigglie reported to Council on the Mayhugh Property.

Bernard C. Proctor reported to Council on several projects
and gave some prices on some proposed Projects the Town is
looking into.

Council discussed sending the request for a Pick Up Truck
to the Property Committee.

Council discussed the purchase of the new Garbage Truck and
requested David Garrett, Jr., to come to the next meeting.
in February.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.
