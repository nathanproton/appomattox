Regular Meeting - September 13,

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on
Monday, September 13, 1993, at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor Spiggle
presiding.

Those members present: S. E. Lawson, H. W. Conner, R. L. Bass,
R. W. Mitchell, S. E. Carter, and W. H. Carson.

Others present: Lisa Pritcher, Dennis Gragg, and Bernard
C. Proctor, P.E.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to adopt the Agenda with one addition to New Business
3. St. Anne's Church, and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

Mr. Dennis Gragg, CVPDC, spoke to Council in regard to the
Lynchburg Neighborhood Development Foundation applying for
Funding for low income, first time buyer in the PDC area.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to offer a resolution in support to Lynchburg Neighborhood
Development Foundation and appoint S. E. Lawson.to the Advisory
Committee to represent the Town and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

voted to accept the recommendation of the Planning Commission
and approve the Conditional Use Permit for Steve and Laura
