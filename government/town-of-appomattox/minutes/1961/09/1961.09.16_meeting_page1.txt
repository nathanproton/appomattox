content: SEPTEMBER MEETING The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's office on September 16, 1961 at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor Dr. S. R. Lawson presiding. The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, H. E. Pack, T. R. Gilbert, and Ivey Holland. Motion made by H. E. Pack, seconded by Ivey Holland, that the Property Committee be authorized to secure bids and a new Police Car and dispose of the 1957 Ford and 1955 Chevrolet cars. Motion carried. Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, that the recommendations of Wiley Wilson on the sewer line be carried out as soon as possible with the present Town personnel and additional help if needed. Motion carried. Motion made by T. R. Gilbert, seconded by H. E. Pack, that the Council give the Mayor the authority to get Mr. Jones from the Lynchburg Water Dept. to come down here and help us with our water problems and make the necessary recommendations. Motion carried. Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by H. E. Pack that Mrs. C. H. Robinson be elected as President of the Council. Motion carried. Motion made by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, seconded by T.
