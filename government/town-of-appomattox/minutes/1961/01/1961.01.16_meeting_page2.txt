content: Motion was made by H. B. Pack seconded by T. R. Gilbert, That the following resolution be adopted: WHEREAS, The Highway Commission has in the past allocated funds and constructed a curb and gutter and sidewalk project on Route 460 in the Town of Appomattox from Church Street (Route 727) to School Road (Route 9021), and; WHEREAS, The Highway Commission has at present an allocation for construction and will construct a curb and gutter and sidewalk project on Route 460 in the Town of Appomattox from School Road (Route 9021) to the West Corporate Limits of the Town of Appomattox, and; WHEREAS, There is an urgent need to extend the curb and gutter and sidewalk construction on Route 460 in the Town of Appomattox from Church Street (Route 727) to the East Corporate Limits of Appomattox, in order to provide safety and convenience to the users of this road. NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, That the Highway Commission give every consideration possible to an additional allocation to continue the reconstruction of Route 460 through the Town of Appomattox to extend to the East Corporate Limits. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That a copy of this resolution be sent to Mr. H. G. Harris, Highway Commissioner, Mr. C. Watkins, Jr., Commissioner for Lynchburg District; Mr. H. H. Phillips, District Engineer; and Mr. C. A. Palmer, Resident Engineer. Motion carried. There being no further business the meeting adjourned. Signed Dr. S. R. Lawson, Mayor Signed C. J. Hunter, Clerk
