content: OCTOBER MEETING
The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's office on October 16, 1961 at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson presiding. The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, C. Lewis McDearmon, J. T. Almond, E. B. Pack, T. R. Gilbert and Ivey Holland. 

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read. 

Motion made by C. Lewis McDearmon, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, that the Town put in a 4 inch water line from 160 up Lucy Street, about half way up the street and put in a 2 inch fire hydrant. Motion carried.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned. 

Signed A. R. Lawson, Mayor 
Dr. S. R. Lawson

Signed C. J. Hunter, Clerk 
C. J. Hunter
