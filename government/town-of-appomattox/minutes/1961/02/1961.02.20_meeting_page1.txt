content: FEBRUARY 1961

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on February 20, 1961, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor Chas. T. Moses, Jr. Presiding.

The following members were present: Dr. S. R. Lawson; H. B. Peck; J. T. Almond; Ivey Holland; T. R. Gilbert, and Mrs. C. H. Robinson.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

A motion was made by Dr. Lawson, seconded by Ivey Holland, That 
be it resolved by the Town Council of the Town of Appomattox, Virginia, 
That boats and house trailers shall be taxed as personal property
beginning January 1, 1961, at the rate of $1.00 per $100.00 valuation.
The same rate as all personal property in the Town of Appomattox.
Motion carried.
