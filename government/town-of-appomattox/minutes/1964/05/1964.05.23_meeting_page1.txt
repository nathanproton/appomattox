content: COMMITTEE MEETING

A special meeting of the Water, Sewer and Finance Committees met in the Mayor's Office, on May 23, 1964, at 1:00 o'clock, with Mayor S. R. Lawson, presiding.

The following members were present: Dick Wingfield, Burke Guill, T. R. Gilbert.

These committees were called to meet with Mr. Elliott.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor
Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk

JUNE MEETING

The Regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on June 15, 1964, at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor S. R. Lawson presiding. The following members were present: Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, T. R. Gilbert, Mrs. C. H. Robinson, R. L. Wingfield.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion made by R. L. Wingfield, seconded by Ivey Holland, that the Town donate 500.00 to the Appomattox Heritage Corporation, pending on the advise of the Town Attorney. Motion carried.

Motion made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Burke Guill, that the Town grant W. E. Jamerson the privilege of tapping on the Town Water and sewer system, (its out of Town on Route 460). This only includes one business place. Motion carried.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor
Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk
