On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted
to deny the request for a donation to the Appomattox Rescue
Squad due to the fact that the Town has already spend funds
for the engineering on the connection for the proposed new
Squad Building on Route 460, and ail those present voting

aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council

voted to approve the Tour Dupont Race in Appomattox on

May 7th and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council,
the meeting adjourned.
