REGULAR MEETING - JANUARY 9, 1995

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was
held on Monday, January 9, 1995 at 7:30 PM with Mayor Spiggle
presiding.

Those present: R. W. Mitchell, H. W. Conner, S. E. Lawson,
R. L. Bass, S. E. Carter, and W. H. Carson.

Others present: Cindy Smith, D. S. Nash, and Bobbie Mullins,
Clerk. “s

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to approve the Consent Agenda with one addition to New
Business: 5. Tour Dupont Race, and all those present voting

aye. Motion carried.

David Nash appeared before Council to discuss his proposal
for ten more connections at his trailer park. Council
reinterated their decision that we can not’: reserve capacity
in advance, and he will need to ask for each connection

on an as need basis.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council

voted to refer to the Street Committee the request for a Street
Light on Morris Avenue between Lombardy and Dogwood Street

and the request for a cul de sac on Cawthorne Street, and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to accept the recommendation of the Property Committee
regarding renovations of the Council Chambers and Storage Room
as follows:

2. Recommend paneling the Council Chambers

in 1978.
