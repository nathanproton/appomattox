REGULAR-MEETING = February 137-1996 2iR5

The recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town
Council was held on Monday, November 20, 1995 at 8:30 PM
with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: S. E. Lawson, Marvin Mitchell,
R. L. Bass, and S. E. Carter. Absent - R. W. Mitchell

Others present: John Wilson, Brod Pack, and Bobbie Mullins,
Clerk.

Mayor Spiggle ~reported he had attended the Planning Commission
Public Hearing and they have recommended the approval of

Brod Pack's request for Re-Zoning of his property on the back
portion of the lot from R-2 to B-l.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted
to approve the request from Mr. Brod Pack to rezone his
property from R-2 to B-l and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

Mr. Steve Lawson had received a telephone call from Mr.
Jim Sefter to put his name on the list of people who would
like to fill the unexpired term of W. H. Carson.

Mr. John L. Wilson, came before Council to state his
qualifications to serve should he be voted to fill the
vacancy.

Council members decided to set a Workshop meeting on
December 4, 1995 to discuss the people who have expressed a
desire to serve on Council and hear from the candidates.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.
