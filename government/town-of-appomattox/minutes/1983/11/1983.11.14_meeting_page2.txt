content: On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted to deny the request for a donation to the Spout Spring Ruritan Club and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council voted to deny the request from Mr. Jones at Spout Spring to buy the scales at the Depot and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mr. Britton reported that the Board of Supervisors were requesting use of the Back Room for the Recreation Committee. Council took no action at this time.

Mr. Lawson suggests as Chairman of the Fire Committee, the tentative date of December 5, 1983 at Jeannes Restaurant for the Annual Supper.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel and all members present voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

Council reconvened into regular session at 9:15 P.M.

Mr. Carter reported that a Commission has been appointed to study the utilization of Town forces in non-highway construction projects.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:30 P.M.
