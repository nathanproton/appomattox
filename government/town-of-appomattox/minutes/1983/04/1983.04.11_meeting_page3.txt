content: On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council voted to apply for a litter Grant in conjunction with the County and the Town of Pamplin and adopt the following resolution: (Copy filed in Council file dated April 1983)

Carter-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Noble-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted to not accept the proposal from Michie Company on the Town Code, and all members present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle reported that the Appomattox Chamber of Commerce is asking for a donation of $250.00 this year for the brochures.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted to donate $250.00 to the Appomattox Chamber of Commerce and all members voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 10:00 P.M.
