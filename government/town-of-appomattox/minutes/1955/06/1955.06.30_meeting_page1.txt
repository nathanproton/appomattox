content: The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on June 30, 1955, with Mayor Chas. F. James presiding. The following members were present: G. E. Smith, Mrs. C. H. Robinson, T. Hunter Dickerson, J. T. Almond and Chas. T. Moses, Jr.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved as read.

Mr. G. W. Carpenter, from the League of Virginia Municipalities, met with the Council to get the Council to reconsider and become a member for the year of 1955. A motion was made by Chas. T. Moses, Jr., seconded by G. E. Smith that the Town renew their membership in the League of Virginia Municipalities at an annual fee of $25.00. Motion carried.

A motion was made by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, seconded by G. E. Smith that the Town pick up garbage two days a week beginning June 27 and continuing until September 15, 1955. The days for the pick up will be Monday and Thursday of each week. Motion carried.

Motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by Chas. T. Moses, Jr., that Mr. Mitchell's new hours be from 5:00 o'clock P.M. to 7:00 o'clock A.M. The watchman old clock be cut out as of June 21, 1955, also a refund to be made to the ones that paid for the clock. Motion carried.

A motion was made by Chas. T. Moses, Jr., seconded by G. E. Smith that the Town put in a water meter at all places that are using town water. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of July 18, 1955.
