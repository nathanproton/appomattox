content: The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on October 17, 1955, at 7:30 P.M., with the President, Mrs. C. H. Robinson presiding. The following members were present: G. E. Smith, J. C. Caldwell, T. Hunter Dickerson, J. T. Irby. The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved as read. A motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by J. T. Irby that the resignation of Dr. James Mayor be tabled until the next meeting on November 21, 1955. Motion carried. A letter from Mr. Wootten regarding the parking in the Street leading to his Mill was turned over to the Street Committee for their recommendation. Motion was made by J. T. Irby seconded by G. E. Smith that the Property Committee make arrangements to purchase seven (7) chairs for the Mayor's Office. Motion carried. There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of November 21, 1955.
