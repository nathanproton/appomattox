# REGULAR MEETING – August 11, 2003

Page 2 of 3


The Appomattox Town Council held a recessed and reconvened meeting on Monday,
August 25, 2003, at 6:00 PM with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: C. Lewis McDearmon, Norman Mayberry, W. H. Carson,
Marvin H. Mitchell, Joyce Bennett, and Steve Lawson.

Others present were: Chad Denton, Times Virginian, Robin Wood, Attorney, ed
Cleland, Engineer, William M. Phillips, Town Attorney, Bif Johnson, Hurt & Proffitt,
Charles Garrett, David T. Garrett, Jr., Town Manager, Roxanne Paulette, Deputy Clerk,
and Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council voted to approve the
Consent Agenda with the addition of the Chamber of Commerce Agreement to
Unfinished Business, and all those present voting aye. Motion Carried.

Ona motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council voted to convene in
Closed Session in accordance with Section 2.2.3711 Subsection A, Item 7, of the State
Code, for the purpose of consultation with legal counsel regarding specific legal matters
requiring legal advice by such counsel.

Bennett-aye Carson-aye McDearmon-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry —aye Lawson-aye

Now, Therefore, Be It Resolved that the Appomattox Town Council hereby certifies that
to the best of each members knowledge: 1. Only public business matters lawfully
exempted from open meetings requirements by Virginia Law were discussed in closed
meeting to which this Certificate Resolution applies, and 2. Only such public business
matters as were identified in the motion convening the closed meeting were heard,
discussed, or considered by this Council.

Vote:

Lawson ~- aye Mayberry —aye Mitchell -aye
McDearmon — aye Carson -aye Bennett -aye
Nays: 0

Absent: 0

Motion carried.
