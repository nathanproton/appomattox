Council Meeting — June 9, 2003

Mayor Spiggle informed Council that we have received word from VDOT that it has
approved the renaming of the Town’s portion of the By-Pass in memory of C, T. Moses,
Jr. We will plan a small ceremony for this occasion when the signs are installed.

Ona motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. McDearmon, Council voted to convene
in Closed session, pursuant to Section 2.2-3711, A.7 of the Code of Virginia (1950) as
amended, for the purpose of consultation with legal counsel regarding specific legal
matters requiring the provision of legal advise by such.counsel.
