content: OCTOBER MEETING
The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on October 21, 1963, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson presiding.
The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, H. B. Pack, Richard Wingfield, T. R. Gilbert, Ivey Holland.
The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.
A motion was made by Ivey Holland, seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, that a sewer and water permit be approved for J. J. Morris' house trailer, providing the neighbors do not object. Motion carried.
There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of November 18, 1963.
Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor
Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk
