FINANCE COMMITTEE MEETING

The Finance Committee met in the Town Office on December 9, 1963, at 7:30 P.M., with the Chairman, Mr. Ivey Holland presiding. The following members were present: T. R. Gilbert, and H. B. Pack. The purpose of the meeting was to prepare the Town Budget for 1964.

DECEMBER MEETING

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on December 16, 1963, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S. R. Lawson Presiding.

The following members were present: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, T. R. Gilbert, Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, Dick Wingfield and H. B. Pack.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion was made by H. B. Pack seconded by Mrs. C. H. Robinson, That the Town pay one-half of the sewer repair bill for Mr. C. S. Booth if it is determined that the repair is on the Town property. Motion carried.

Motion was made by Ivey Holland seconded by H. B. Pack, That the Town Budget for 1964 be accepted as prepared by the Finance Committee. Motion carried.

Motion made by Richard Wingfield, seconded by Burke Guill, That the Town pay each of the volunteer Firemen $5.00 for services rendered. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of January 20, 1964.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor
Signed J. F. Hunter Clerk
