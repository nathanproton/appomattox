content: MARCH MEETING

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's Office on March 18, 1963, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor S.R. Lawson Presiding.

The following members were present: G. Lewis McDearmon, Mrs. C.H. Robinson, H.B. Pack, Burke Guill, T.R. Gilbert, and Ivey Holland.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion was made by Ivey Holland, seconded by T.R. Gilbert, That the "signs no parking here to corner" be moved back one parking space on Church Street North and South side of Highland Avenue, and one no parking space on Church Street North of Attwood Street. Motion carried.

Motion was made by T.R. Gilbert seconded by G. Lewis McDearmon, That the Town go ahead and fix the sanitary land fill city dump as soon as possible. Motion carried.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned to meet again on the regular meeting date of April 15, 1963.

Signed S.R. Lawson, Mayor
Signed C.J. Hunter, Clerk
