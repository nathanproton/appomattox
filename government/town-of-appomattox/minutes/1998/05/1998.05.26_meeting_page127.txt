Special Call - May 26, 1998

A Special Called Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on Tuesday,
May 26, 1998 at 5:00 PM with Vice-Mayor Bass presiding.

Those members present: Joyce Bennett, Norman Mayberry, Steve Lawson,
and Marvin Mitchell. Absent- Rick Pack.

Others present: David T. Garrett, Jr., and Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.

The purpose of the meeting was to discuss the easement for Parkview Enterprises
which is out of town, and Gray S. Nash, Jeanne N. & James G. Amos, in town for the
Meadowlark Sewer Project.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council voted to approve
the wording for the easement between Gray S. Nash and Jeanne N. Amos and
James G. Amos, which is the twenty two trailers that are already in place.

Lawson-aye Mayberry-aye Mitchell-aye Bennett-aye Pack -absent
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mrs. Bennett, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council voted to approve
the wording of the Parkview Enterprises Inc. Easement for the Meadowlark

Sewer Project which the Council realizes is an exception to the moratorium on out of town

sewer connections due to the need to obtain the easement in order to continue with the
project and not lose the monies for the grant.

Bennett-aye Lawson-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye Pack-absent
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned.

RL. Bass, Vice-Mayor
