Council Minutes - July 13, 1998

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted to leave the
water line on the westside of Route 131, expend the money to correct the hump in the
line at an approximate cost of $36,512.00.

Pack-aye Lawson-aye Bennett-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council voted to pay for the
easement to Mrs. Henrietta Ferguson Preston for the sum of $2275.77.

Pack-aye Lawson-aye Bennett-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Pack, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council voted to expend the
money to replace the Sewer Pump at D. S. Nash at an approximate cost of
$11,228.00.

Pack-aye Lawson-aye Mayberry-aye Mitchell-aye Bennett-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mrs. Bennett, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council voted to table
the question of repair and painting of the Depot:and refer the/question-to the
Property Committee for their study and recommendations and all those present
voting .aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council voted to expend
the monies to have the TV Camera Work done on the sewer lines at an approximate
cost of $7696.90.

Pack-aye Lawson-aye Mayberry-aye Mitchell-aye Bennett-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before.Council, the meeting adjourned.
