Regular Meeting - August 10, 1998

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on Monday,
August 10, 1998 at 7:30 PM with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present were: R. L. Bass, S. E. Lawson, Joyce Bennett,
Marvin Mitchell, Jimmy Mayberry, and Rick Pack.

Others present were: David T. Garrett, Jr., William Phillips, Town Attorney,
and Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Marvin Mitchell, seconded by Norman Mayberry, Council
voted to approve the Consent Agenda, and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

Steve Lawson reported that the Street Committee had met and recommended
that the sidewalk be replaced in front of the Middle School. Also, they
recommend that Linden Street be closed as previously discussed.

On a motion by Steve Lawson, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council voted to close
the section of Linden Street from its intersection with South Church Street, for
a distance of 250 feet and replace the sidewalk in front of the Middle School.

Bass-aye Pack-aye Bennett-aye Lawson-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Marvin Mitchell, seconded by R. L. Bass, Council voted to
remodel a specified area of the Town Office to include the “old Mayor's
office” and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr Mayberry, Council voted to
approve the ratification of the amendments to the Central Virginia Planning
District Commission By-Laws and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council voted to table
Item 3, Unfinished Business, Car Wash, until the next regular meeting and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted to allow
the Fire Department to meet in the Council Chambers two nights a month
and all thage present voting aye. Motion carried.
