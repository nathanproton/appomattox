Council Minutes
March 23, 1998

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted to adopt the
Procurement Policy as it pertains to the purchase of goods and services for the Town
of Appomattox, with the changes as recommended by the Ordinance Committee.

See attached copy.

Bass-aye Lawson-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye Pack-aye Bennett-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council voted to extend the
Police Services Agreement for two years, at an annual fee of $80,000.00

with payment being made twice a year to the County of Appomattox, and all those
present voting aye. Motion.carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted not to join the
Industrial Development Authority, and not be a part of the Regional Industrial

Park, but participate with the construction of water and sewer service to the selected
site on a 75-25 % basis with the County of Appomattox.

Bass-aye Pack-aye Mayberry-aye Mitchell-aye Bennett-aye Lawson-nay
Motion carried by a 5 to 1 vote.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned,

Bobbie H. Mullins

MAYOR

Ronald C. Spiggle
