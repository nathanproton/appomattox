qqeA SPECIAL CALL OF COUNCIL - April 30, 1998

A Special Call of Town Council was held on Thursday, April 30, 1998,
at 7:30 PM with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: R. L. Bass, Norman Mayberry, Marvin H. Mitchell,
Rick Pack, Steve E. Lawson, and Joyce Bennett.

Others present were: David T. Garrett, Jr., and Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.

The purpose of the meeting was to discuss the purchase of the property
owned by Patrick Walker in conjunction with the purchase of the
Mickey and Sandra Martin property.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council voted to purchase
both pieces of property, one from Patrick Walker for $10,000.00 and the
other from Mickey and Sandra Martin for purchase of $35,000.00 to. add

to the property at the Town Shop and create more storage area and

future expansion.
