content: Committee Meeting

The Fire Committee met on February 18, 1972 in the Municipal Building at 7:30 P.M.

Present were M. L. Webb, Chairman, and T. E. Conner.

Mayor Pack and some of the members of the Fire Department were present.

A proposed revision of the Fire Department was discussed.

M. L. Webb, Chairman
