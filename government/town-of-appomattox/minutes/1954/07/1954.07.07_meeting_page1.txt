content: A special meeting of the Appomattox Town Council met in the Mayor's office, July 7, 1954, at 7:30 P.M., with Mayor Chas. F. James presiding.

The following members were present: G. E. Smith, C. C. Poe, Mrs. H. C. H. Robinson, J. T. Almond and D. A. Christian, III.

A motion was made by G. E. Smith, seconded by C. C. Poe, that all property outside of the Appomattox corporate limits that have sewer service and connections be charged $15.00 tapping fee plus all cost to the Town sewer lines, plus $0.50 per $100.00 valuation of the property served by sewage, which is the same fee paid by the property owners that live in the Town of Appomattox, to be effective as of July 7, 1954. Motion carried.

Signed: Chas. F. James, Mayor
Signed: C. F. Hunter, Clerk
