Council Minutes
October 15, 1996

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to deny the passage of the
proposed amendment to the Town Code Section 46-2, Firing of Weapons, in Town.

Lawson-nay Bass-nay
Mitchell-nay Carter-nay
Pack-nay Mayberry-nay

Motion passed to deny passage.
There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 8:50 PM.
