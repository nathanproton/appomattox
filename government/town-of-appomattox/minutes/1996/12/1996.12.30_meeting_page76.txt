RECESSED AND RECONENED

The recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town Council
was held on Monday, December 30, 1996, at 7:00 PM with Mayor
Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: S. E. Lawson, R. L. Bass, S. E. Carter,
and Norman Mayberry, and Rick Pack. Absent-Marvin Mitchell

Others present: Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.
The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. R. L. Bass, seconded by S. E. Carter, Council
voted to adopt the 1997 Town Budget as submitted, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to
set the Real Estate Tax Rate at .15 per $100 of assessed value
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to
set the Personal Property Tax Rate at .55 per $100 of assessed
value and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted
to set the Merchants Capital at .55 per $100.00 of assessed
value and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Pack, Council
voted to authorize Mayor Spiggle to execute the ISTEA Agreement
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On.a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council
voted to set the 1997 Salaries for Town Employees as previously
set forth in the Budget and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

There being no further business, to come before Council,
the meeting adjourned.
