presiding. Note: The regular meeting date should have been
January 8th but due to weather conditions, it had to he
postponed.

Mr. David T. Garrett, Jr., and Kent Hodges presented a report
to Town Council on the additional capacity at the Sewer Piant
and Lagoon that can be done with a few minor changes. These
changes would have to be approved by the DEQ and the Health
Department. The recommended changes would costs 23,000.00, of
which $4,100.00 is the engineers fee.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council
voted to pursue the possibility that this engineers proposal
presents, provided the changes are approved by the DEQ and
the Health Department and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded. by Mr. Carter, Council voted
to appoint Mr. William M. Phillips, as the Town of Appomattox
