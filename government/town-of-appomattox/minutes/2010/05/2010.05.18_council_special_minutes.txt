1  Appomattox Town Council

Special Call Meeting
May 18, 2010

At the Special Call Meeting of the Appomattox Town Council held on May 18, 2010 at 7:00 pm
in the Council Chambers, Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox,
Virginia, there present were Mayor Paul D. Harvey, presiding.

Council members present:  Bryan J. Baine, N. H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr.,
and John T. “Plicky” Williams.  Absent:  Karl E. Carter and Jennifer Jamerson-Scruggs.

Also in attendance were Jeff Elder, Stacey Wilkes, Bill Smith, Cliff Harvey, Nina Beasley, Hazel
Thomas, Bill and June Goodrich, Mary Lou Spiggle, Joyce Bennett, Timmy Garrett, Ronnie
Lankford, Chad Millner, Bart S. Van Nieuwenhuise, Frank Wright and Roxanne W. Paulette,
Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order and welcome those in attendance.

Mayor Harvey cited the purpose of the meeting:
1.

Discussion and authorization to proceed with the Public Hearing for the FY 2011 Budget
for the Town of Appomattox.

2.

Discussion and authorization to proceed with the Public Hearing for the equalization of
monthly water and sewer billing rates (in town and out of town) for FY 2011.

On a motion by Councilmember Williams, seconded by Councilmember Mayberry, Council
voted to advertise a Public Hearing for the FY 2011 Budget for the Town of Appomattox.  All
members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

On a motion by Councilmember McDearmon, seconded by Councilmember Williams, Council
voted to advertise a Public Hearing for the equalization of monthly water and sewer billing rates
and the availability and connection fees.

On a motion by Councilmember Mayberry, seconded by Councilmember Williams, Council
voted to adjourn at 7:12 p.m.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried.

Roxanne W. Paulette, CMC

Paul D. Harvey, Mayor
