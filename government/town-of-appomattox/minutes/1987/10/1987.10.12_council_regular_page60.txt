October 12, 1987 meeting

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to hold

a Public Hearing at 7:00 P.M. at the November Council meeting on the
request from Michael Andrusky for a conditional use permit on Atwood Street.
On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to table

the Court Street parking problem until the November meeting and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

The Council meeting recessed and will reconvene on Monday, October 18, 1987
at 7:00 P.M. in the Meeting Room.
