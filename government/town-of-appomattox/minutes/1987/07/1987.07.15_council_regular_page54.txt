On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted
to adopt the following resolution on the south side of Church Street
drainage problem: ~

WHEREAS, the Downtown area has changed with
the addition of new parking lots and
street repairs; and

WHEREAS, the large storm drains are
draining into smaller existing systems south
of the Norfolk-Southern Railroad; and

WHEREAS, the area south of the railroad
known as Chinatown, has been overwhelmed with
water several times over the years; and

WHEREAS, the Virginia Department of Highways
owns and maintains the majority of the
streets in the Town; and

WHEREAS, the Town would like to see this
problem solved;

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Town
Council of Appomattox request the Virginia
Department of Highways to re-examine the
storm sewer study made in 1979-80 and
re-examine the existing problem of storm
drainage; and that the Town will participate
in the cost of the study according to Highway
Department's regulations; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Town
Council is further interested in a storm
water project to alleviate this problem
as it now exists and would participate in.
said project as stated in the Highway
Department's Rules and Regulations.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye Mitchell-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr, Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted to
not participate in repairs to the drainage ditch on the Annie Laurie
Babcock property and all those present voting:.aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned at 6:20 P.M.
