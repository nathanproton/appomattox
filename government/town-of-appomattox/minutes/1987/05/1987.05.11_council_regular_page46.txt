Motion carried unanimously.

Bernard Proctor, Engineer, presented a proposal for the Availability
Fees for McDonald's Corporation. (See attached copy).

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted
to accept the proposal for the Availability Fee for McDonald's
and all those members present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to
grant Al Sears and Steve Lawson's request for a water connection

on their property off Morton Lane, and all those members present
voting aye. Motion carried. (See attached copy)

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted
to table the Meals Tax Ordinance and Item 2, Retirement at 55 until
the June meeting. Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the

meeting adjourned. ‘
