September 14, 1987

On a motion by Mr. Carter, Seconded by Mr Webb, Council voted to
accept Marion Nash's request to include her property in the boundary
proposal provided that she bear the expense of the survey and any other
expenses incurred for including her property.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Mitchell-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle reported to Council on the latest development
in the proposed History Junction Shopping Center.

It was general consensus of Town Council that if the second proposed

Shopping Center wishes to connect to Town Water and Sewer, they will

need to pay 50% of the costs of connection and installation fees.

It was also decided that they could not allow a laundromat to connect
due to the water usuage.

Council discussed the recurring problems with the Cable TV System.
It was requested that a representative meet with Council at the
next regular meeting.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted to
accept the bid from Bohannon Electrical, Heating and Air Conditioning
for 2 - 3 Ton Lennox Heat Pumps to serve the Council Room and the front
offices at a price of $5,760.00.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Mitchell-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to came before Council, the
meeting adjourned.

R. C. Spiggle
