content: Special Committee for Cable TV

A meeting of the Cable TV Committee was held on October 9, 1978 at 6:30 P.M. in the Appomattox Town Office.

Those present were Chairman, Thomas Lawson, R. C. Hubbard, and Mr. R. L. Bass. Also present was W. R. Britton, Jr., Town Manager.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard to recommend to Council the adoption of the Attached Ordinance for a Cable TV Franchise

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned.

Thomas Lawson

Chairman
