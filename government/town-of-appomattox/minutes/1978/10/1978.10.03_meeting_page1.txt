A meeting of the Property Committee was held on October 3, 1978 at 5:00 P.M. in the Appomattox Town Office.

Those members present were T. E. Conner, Chairman, Samuel Carter, and Mr. T. W. Lawson.

Also present was Town Manager, W. R. Britton, Jr.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Lawson, all all voting aye, the Committee recommends to the Finance Committee the purchasing of a leaf collection system in the FY 79 Budget.

There being no further business the meeting adjourned.

Thomas E. Conner, Chairman
