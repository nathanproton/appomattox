content: Mr. Lawson requested that the Committees begin investigating the possible changes to the Town Code and revising the Town Zoning Ordinance.

Mr. Lawson made the motion, seconded by Mr. Carter, to elect Mr. R. L. Bass, as Vice-Mayor to the Town Council.

Carter - aye Hubbard - aye Lawson - aye Gordon - aye Conner - aye

Mr. Conner requested a letter be written to Mr. Pittman commending the work on the Streets in Town.

Mr. Bass made the motion, seconded by Mr. Conner, with all members voting aye, to purchase notebooks for the Council Members.

At 10:00 Mayor Spiggle, called for an Executive Session:

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Bass, to raise the salary of the Clerk from $6725.00 to $7560.00 effective as of September 1st, 1978. All members voted aye.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned.

Ronald C. Spiggle Mayor
