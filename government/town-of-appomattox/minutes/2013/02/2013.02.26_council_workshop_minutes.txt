1  Workshop Meeting
    Tuesday, February 26, 2013

The Appomattox Town Council held a Workshop meeting on Tuesday, February 26,
2013 at 7:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street,
Appomattox, Virginia with Mayor Harvey presiding.

Members present: Timothy W. “Timmy” Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Mary Lou
Spiggle, N.H. “Jimmy” Mayberry, Steven T. “Steve” Conner and Claudia G. Puckette.

Others present: Jeff Elder, Stephanie James (Times Virginian), Patricia Garrett, Katrina
Koerting (News & Advance), Carlton Duck, Stacey Wilkes, Town Treasurer; C. William
Gillespie, Interim Town Manager; Roxanne W. Paulette, Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the workshop meeting to order.

Mr. Gillespie provided an updated of the Capital Improvement Plan.  The Physical
Development Committee reviewed the Capital Improvement Plan for the next 5 years.

Mr. Gillespie gave an update on the Inflow & Infiltration camera project.  The project is
being done in an effort to close the Trickling Filter Plant.  Hopefully, the Town will be
under construction for repairing the I&I next year and building the pump station the
following year.

Council discussed the FY 2014 budget process, fixed asset policy and procurement
ordinance.

Committee Updates

Finance & Planning
Mr. McDearmon reported that the committee discussed a possible livestock “chicken”
ordinance.  A consensus was not reached in committee.

Physical Development Committee
Mr. Mayberry reported that the committee met tonight and discussed the Capital
Improvement Plan, Inflow & Infiltration project, Water and Sewer revenue projections
and bids for the new pickup truck (Sewer).

Staff Updates
Clerk – None

Treasurer – The new software is transitioning well.

Town Manager - None

Council Concerns
None

Vote:  Garrett – aye, McDearmon – aye, Spiggle – aye, Mayberry – aye, Conner – aye,
Puckette – aye.

Motion carried.

On a motion by Councilmember McDearmon, seconded by Councilmember Garrett,
