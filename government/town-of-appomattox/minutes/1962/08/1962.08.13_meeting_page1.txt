content: AUGUST MEETING

The regular meeting of the Town Council met in the Mayor's office on August 13, 1962 at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor S. R. Lawson presiding. The following members were present: G. Lewis McDearmon, Burke R. Guill, Ivey Holland, W. B. Pack, T. R. Gilbert and Mrs. C. K. Robinson.

The minutes of the previous meeting were read and approved as read.

Motion made by Burke R. Guill, seconded by T. R. Gilbert, that the motion passed by the Council on July 16, 1962, that the town hire M. A. Stanley, be rescinded. Motion Carried.

Motion made by W. B. Pack, seconded by T. R. Gilbert, that the Town employ a part-time policeman and he worked at the discretion of the Mayor. Motion Carried.

Motion made by G. Lewis McDearmon, seconded by Mrs. C. K. Robinson that the Town get Mr. White a winter blouse. Motion Carried.

Motion made by G. Lewis McDearmon, seconded by Ivey Holland, that the Town appoint a Justice of the Peace to write warrants for the Town. Motion Carried.

Motion made by W. B. Pack, seconded by Mrs. C. K. Robinson, that the Council give the Mayor authority to sell the parking meters. Voting for the motion: W. B. Pack, Mrs. Robinson, T. R. Gilbert, Burke Guill Voting against: Ivey Holland, G. Lewis McDearmon. Motion Carried.

There being no further business the meeting was adjourned.

Signed S. R. Lawson Mayor

Signed C. J. Hunter Clerk
