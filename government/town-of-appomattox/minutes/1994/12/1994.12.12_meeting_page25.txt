COUNCIL MINUTES
December 12, 1994

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council

voted to deny the request from Mr. & Mrs. Earl Austin to buy the
property on Route 604 in the Promise Land Subdivision and

all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to refer to the Ordinance Committee the Joint Enforcement
Compact on Vehicle Decal Ordinance for their study and
recommendation and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council
voted to not apply for the ISTEA Grant this year and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to recess and reconvene on December 29, 1994 at 4:30
PM.-to adopt the 1995-Budget-and- all those present-voting-aye~ ~
Motion carried. ,

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.
