Council Minutes
March 14, 1994

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council
voted to designate the weeks fo March 28 thru April 1, and
April 4 to April 8, as Spring Clean Up Weeks and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to refer the request for a new Computer to Mr. Carter's
Property Committee and bring back some recommendations to
Council, and ali those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to recess and reconvene on March 23, 1994 at 7:30

PM to complete the Agenda and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned.
