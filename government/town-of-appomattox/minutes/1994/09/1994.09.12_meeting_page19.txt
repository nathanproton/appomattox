Council Minutes - September 12, 1994

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to refer the matter of the VRS to the Personnel Committee

for their study and recommendations.
Mr. Carter withdrew his motion

Mr. Mitchell withdrew his second
Motion rescinded

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council

voted to adopt the following resolution concerning VRS benefits

increase of 3% for retirees future and prior and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried. See attached copy.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to set the Clean Up Weeks for October 24th thru 28th
and November lst thru 4th and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried.

Mr. Bass brought Council up to date on the sewer line on
Ethel Street/Lizzie Street. David and the Town Crew are
installing the Sewer Line.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council

voted to in accordance with State Code, Section 2.1-344, to go

into Executive Session to discuss Land Acquisition and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted unanimously to return to regular session and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

I, Bobbie Mullins, Clerk of the Appomattox Town Council, do

certify that Land Acquisition and Land Acquisition only
was discussed.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned.
