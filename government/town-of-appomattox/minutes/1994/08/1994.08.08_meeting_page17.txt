Council minutes - August 8, 1994

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to enter into a twelve month contract with the
Division of Motor Vehicles to handle the Licensing: Agency
provided all stipulations by the Town are met and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.
