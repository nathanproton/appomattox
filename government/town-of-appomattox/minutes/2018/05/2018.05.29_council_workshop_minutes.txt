1  Appomattox Town Council
Council Workshop Meeting
May 29, 2018

The Appomattox Town Council held a Council Workshop meeting on Tuesday, May 29, 2018 at
6:00 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox, Virginia with
Mayor Harvey, presiding.

Members present:  Timothy W. Garrett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Jr., Mary Lou Spiggle, M. Erin
Finch, Steven T. Conner and Claudia G. Puckette.

Others present:  James Boyce, Jonathan Garrett, Kim Ray, Town Treasurer; Roxanne W. Casto,
Clerk of Council.

Mayor Harvey called the meeting to order.

Staff presentations – None

Comments from the public –

Mayor Harvey stated that the Town needs to pass a balanced budget.

There were no other public comments.  Mayor Harvey closed the public hearing.

Council Concerns –
Mr. Conner stated that he has received some complaints or concerns of the company conducting
the line lateral inflow and infiltration construction driving off the highway and into the yards of
homeowners.  Mr. Elder and Mr. Shanaberger will investigate this complaint further with the
contractor.

Mrs. Puckette would appreciate it if the Town would send Congressman Tom Garrett a thank
you letter for his service and wish him the best for his future.  Council gave consensus to send
Congressman Garrett a thank you letter.

Staff Reports –
Mr. Elder – none - absent

Mrs. Casto reported that there will be a vacancy on the Town Economic Development Authority
and the Board of Zoning Appeals as of July 1, 2018.  Council members need to consider
replacements for both of these boards.

Mrs. Ray provided the April 30, 2018 Financial Report.  She is also currently examining Utility
debts, Personal Property and Business License delinquencies.

Mrs. Earhart provided training and consultation in the middle of May.  She discussed a few of
the Town’s unique situations and assisted with satisfying several of the auditor’s questions.  Mrs.
Ray thanked the Town for allowing her the opportunity to use Mrs. Earhart.

Mrs. Ray will explore the Virginia Municipal League investment products.  She will be looking
at other options for the Town’s investments.

Mrs. Ray stated that near the end of the summer or early fall, she will be looking into new
financial software packages.  Mrs. Earhart is also willing to assist with closing out the Town’s
books in the early fall.

The FY 2017 Annual audit – The auditors have one more question and Mrs. Ray is waiting for
some guidance from Mrs. Earhart.

Mr. Shanaberger – none - absent

On a motion by Mr. McDearmon, seconded by Ms. Spiggle, Council voted to adjourn at 6:30
p.m.  All members present voting aye.  Motion carried 6-0.

Roxanne W. Casto, MMC
Clerk of Council

Paul D. Harvey, Mayor
