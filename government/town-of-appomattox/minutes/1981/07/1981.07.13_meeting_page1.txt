content: On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to go into Executive Session to discuss Personnel problems.

Council reconvened in regular session at 10:45 P.M.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council voted to approve the raise given to Mr. Britton by the County Board of Supervisors. (See copy of letter in Council file dated July 13, 1981).

Carson-aye Conner-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye Motion carried unanimously.

Mr. Britton was requested to get the necessary information and bring the matter back to Council for their action and to honor the agreement with May & Associates, Inc., as previously agreed upon by Council. (See Council file dated July 13, 1981).

There being no further business to come before Council the meeting adjourned at 10:45 P.M.
