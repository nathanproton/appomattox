Carson-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye Conner-Abstaining

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to adopt a Continual Resolution for the Virginia Department of Highways on the Stop Sign installation in the Town of Appomattox. (See copy in Council file dated March 1981).

Carson-aye Conner-aye Bass-aye Lawson-aye Noble-aye Hubbard-aye Motion carried unanimously.

Council discussed Phillip Seay's request for the Depot and felt that they would stay with a previous decision and deny his request.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:00 A.M.
