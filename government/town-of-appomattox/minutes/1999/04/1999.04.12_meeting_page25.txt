Council Minutes - April 12, 1999

Mayor Spiggle referred Town Council to Mrs. Roxanne
Paulette’s report on the Town’s Y2K Status.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council
voted to grant the request from the Chamber of Commerce to
delay the rent payments until July 1999, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson,
Council voted to expend and appropriate the $3570.00 for
the Sewer Lab Tests and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to appoint Mayor Spiggle as the Town’s
representative on the joint Public Safety Committee and all
those present voting aye. Motion carrie.d

The three drilled wells on the Central Viginia Electric
Property did not produce enough water for the Town.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council
voted to grant the necessary permits to Tiny Angel Day Care
for a gospel sing to be held on July 10th and waive the
fees, and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned at

8:55 PM.

Ronald C. Spigg!
