Council Minutes - November 8, 1999

Mr. Mayberry, Chairman of the Sewer Committee, reported the
Committee recommends approval of the Reed Bed Systems
Agreement with several changes to include a “hold harmless
agreement” for both the Contractor and the Town, and that
Sigmatron is the parent company.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mrs. Bennett,
Council voted to approve the Reed Bed Systems Agreement
with the corrections as noted and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried. (See attached copy)

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. McDearmon,
Council voted to approve the two out of town water
connections as requested by R. C. Stephens and Delphia
Beringer and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to set Fall Clean Up Weeks as November 15th
thru November 19 and November 29° thru December 3° and
all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

Council referred the question of the Phone System to the
Property Committee for their study. The Committee
requested we obtain a second price.
