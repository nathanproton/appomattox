Council Minutes -— January 11, 1999

The Street Committee has not had a chance to meet on the
request for the Street light on Bandana Street

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson,
Council voted to deny the request from the Actions
Committee, due to the Town’s obligations and commitments
for 1999 and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Lawson, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to approve the water connection of Lynn
Bentley on Route 677, Bible Baptist Church Road and all
those present voting aye.

Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to set the out of Town Sewer Rates as
presented and all those present voting aye. Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mrs Bennett,
Council voted to appoint Charles E. Garrett as Chief

Wastewater Plant Operator and set his salary accordingly.

Lawson-aye Bennett~aye Mayberry~-aye Mitchell-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the

meeting adjourned.
