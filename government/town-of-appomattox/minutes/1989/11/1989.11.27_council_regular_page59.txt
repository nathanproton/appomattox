Recessed and Reconvened - November 27, 1989

The recessed and reconvened meeting of the Appomattox Town
Council was held on Monday, November 27, 1989 at 7:00 P.M.
with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: R. L. Bass, S. EH. Carter, H. W. Conner,
W. H. Carson, R. C. Hubbard, and Marvin H. Mitchell.

Others present: Sheriff Gene Richardson, Tina Martin,

Beth Mitchell, Ricardo Rivera, Cameron Trammell, Matt Moore,
Christina Scruggs, Heather Archer, Katie Powell, Shirley
Walton, Keith Webb, and Bobbie Mullins, Clerk.

The Citizens present either spoke against the adoption of
the "Proposed Skateboarding Ordinance" or asked that the
Town/County and Children work out a solution to the
problem, so that the skateboarders will have a place to
skate.

It was the feeling of the Council members that the Town
adopt the same ordinance as the County with a few revisions
as requested during the meeting.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council
voted to adopt the same "Loitering Ordinance" as the County
of Appomattox with the changes as noted. (See attached copy).

Bass-aye Carter-aye Carson-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye
Mitchell-aye Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before the meeting
they adjourned at 7:45 P.M,
