Council minutes - July 10, 1989

Mayor Spiggle was requested to check on the liability
insurance for the Police Department beings as we are now
contracting to the Sheriff's Department for Police Services.

The Mayor reported on the replacement of several sidewalks
in Town in need of repair. One on Oakleigh Avenue and the
other in front of Coflin's Store. We will enter into an
agreement with the Highway Department were we supply the
Materials and they will supply the labor..

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to refer the request for a sidewalk on Court Street
to the Street Committee and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council
voted to advertize their intent to adopt a Flood Plain
Ordinance, at the August 14, 1989 m√©eting, and ail those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to advertize our intent to amend and re-enact Section
13-2, License Generally, and hold a public hearing on
August 14, 1989, at 7:00 P.M.

Bass-aye Conner-aye Carter-aye Mitchell-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council,
the meeting adjourned.

Comat MAYOR
. C. Spiggle
