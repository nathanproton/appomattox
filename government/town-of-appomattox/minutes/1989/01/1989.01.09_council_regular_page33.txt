Council minutes - January 9, 1989

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted
to table Item 6 and 7, of the Agenda until the February meeting
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council voted
to go into Executive Session in accordance with 2.1-344 of

the State Code to discuss Personnel, and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted

to reconvene into regular session, and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried.
