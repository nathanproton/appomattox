February 22, 1989

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to re-instate Lee Jackson-King Day to the list of
holidays in the Town Personnel Policy and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to re-state the Policy on Trash Collection for the Town
of Appomattox. There will be no trash pick-up on the following
holidays:

Christmas Day
Thanksgiving Day
Fourth of July
