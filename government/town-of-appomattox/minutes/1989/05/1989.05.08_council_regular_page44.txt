Council minutes - May 8, 1989 ,

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council
voted to adopt the litter Resolution in conjunction with
the County of Appomattox and the Town of Pamplin and ali
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to
authorize Mayor Spiggle to negotiate on the need

for a Communications Tower on property owned by the Town of
Appomattox, and all those present voting aye. Motion

carried.

Mayor Spiggle reported on the compliants on the water from
several citizens. These have been taken care of.

There being no further business to come before Town Council,
the meeting adjourned.
