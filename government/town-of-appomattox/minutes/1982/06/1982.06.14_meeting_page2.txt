content: On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council voted to continue to deny the request from Mrs. Babcock, and all members present voted aye. Motion carried.

It was general consensus of Council that the "Trash Pick-Up Policy" not be changed at this time.

Mr. Britton explained that plans are under consideration for a Regional Fire Training Facility. Mr. Mitchell, Fire Chief, is on the Committee that is discussing this facility, and would like the feeling of Town Council before he is asked to vote.

The general consensus of Town Council is that having no details of this Facility, that they are not in favor of this facility at this time.

Mayor Spiggle informed Council of a meeting Tuesday, at 3:00 P.M. in the Courtroom to discuss future Crime Legislation.

There will be a meeting on Monday, June 21st at 7:30 P.M. in the Town Office to discuss the Fire Department.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned at 9:00 P.M.
