date: 1969-11-17
content: The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held in the Council Room on November 17, 1969 at 7:30 p.m. with Mayor H. L. Peed presiding. Members present were: Mrs. C. H. Robinson, R. L. Bane, Bruce Grant, Ivey Holland, R. C. Hubbard, and Martin L. Webb.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved.

Mr. Martin and Mr. Thompson appeared before the Council to state that their use of a power-driven saw was necessary in their business and was only used a short period.

Chairman Webb of the Fire Committee was requested to be co-chairman for the Annual Christmas Tree to be held on December 15 at 6:30 p.m.

A meeting of the Finance Committee was tentatively set for December 10 at 7:30 p.m.

The request of Mrs. P. W. Stanley for an easement across the Ball Park to her land was referred to the Property Committee.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed H. L. Peed Mayor
Signed E. P. Scott Clerk
