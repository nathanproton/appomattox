content: Committee Meeting

A meeting of the Fire and Property Committees was held in the Council Room on July 15, 1969 at 8:00 P.M.

Present were: M. L. Webb, R. C. Hubbard, and Mrs. Calvin Robinson, also Mayor H. B. Pack.

The bids from Moses Motor Co., Inc. and Grinnels Motor Co. were checked and discussed.

The low bid of 3731.00 submitted by Moses Motor Co., Inc. on a 1970 Chevrolet Chassis and cab for the joint-owned Town County fire tank truck was accepted.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed: M. L. Webb, Chairman
Signed: R. C. Hubbard, Chairman
Signed: C. S. Booth, Clerk
