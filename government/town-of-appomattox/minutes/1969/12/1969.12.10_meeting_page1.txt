date: 1969-12-10
content: Committee Meeting
A meeting of the Finance Committee of the Town Council was held in the Council Room on December 10, 1969 at 7:30 P.M. Present were: Chairman Ivey Holland, and R. C. Hubbard, Mayor Faek and C. S. Booth were present.
The Committee prepared a proposed budget for 1970 to be presented to the Council.
The meeting adjourned.
