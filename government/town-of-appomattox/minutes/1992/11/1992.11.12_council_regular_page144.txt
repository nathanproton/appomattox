Council Minutes - November 12, 1992

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
reconvened into regular session, and each of the five members
present discussed probable land acquisition and that only,
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council
voted to purchase a Chipper at an approximate purchase price
of $15,000.00 with the County of Appomattox paying one-half
and the Town of Appomattox paying one-half, and ali those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council the

meeting adjourned with a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by
Mr. Lawson and all voting aye.
