Special Call of Council ~- June 30, 1992
The special call of Council was held on Tuesday, June 30,
1992, at 4:30 P.M. with Mayor Spiggle presiding.

Those present: R. C. Hubbard, H. W. Conner, R. L. Bass,
and Marvin Mitchell. Absent - W. H. Carson and S. E. Carter.

Others present: Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk.
On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council

voted to go into Executive Session in accordance with
Section 2.1-344, of the State Code to discuss Personnel

and Personnel only, and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a mottion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to go back into regular session and to certify that

only Personnel and Salaries were discussed during the Executive
Session. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to amend the Police Services Contractual Agreement

for a one time contribution of $8,354.00 towards salaries

of the two officers designated to cover the Town.

Bass-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye Mitchell-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council,
the meeting adjourned.

CLERK
