Council minutes ~ September 14, 1992

ON a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to accept the deletions to the Appomattox Railroad
Depot Renovations in the amount of $16,136.00 making the
total bid figure on the project of $78,644.00 and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carter, seconded by Mr. Bass, Council
voted to compensate Kendra Dunn in the amount of $300.00
for her help with the grant and dinner for two for Mr. &
Mrs. Paul Nix and Mr. & Mrs. Steve Lawson, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Town
Council, the meeting adjourned.
