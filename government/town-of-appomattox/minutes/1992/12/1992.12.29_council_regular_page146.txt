PUBLIC HEARING

A Public Hearing was held on December 29, 1992, at 7:00
PM in the Appomattox Town Office on the Proposed 1993
Budget as advertised in the paper for two weeks.

Those present: S. E. Carter, R. W. Mitchell, H. W. Conner,
5S. E. Lawson, R. L. Bass, Sam Waddell, and Bobbie Mullins,
Clerk.

Mayor Spiggle opened the hearing and asked for public comments.
Sam Waddell, resident, spoke to Council regarding the Budget.
He requested that the Council please keep cost down and

watch expenses.

The hearing was held open until 7:20 P.M. and there being

no other persons wishing to comment, the hearing was closed.

Bobbie H. Mullins, Clerk
