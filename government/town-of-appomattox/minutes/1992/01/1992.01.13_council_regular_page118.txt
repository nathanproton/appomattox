118, Regular meeting - January 13, 1992 .

The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held
on Monday, January 13, 1992, at 7:30 P.M with Mayor
Spiggle presiding.

Those members present: R. C. Hubbard, H. W. Conner,
Marvin Mitchell, R. L. Bass, W. H. Carson, and S. E. Carter.

Others present: Mark Glover, English Construction Company,
David Nash, James Amos, and Grey Nash, Nash Construction
Company, Bill Sherrod, and Bobbie Mullins, Clerk.

The meeting was opened with a prayer.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Mitchell, Council
voted to approve the Consent Agenda with one addition:

6. Handicapped Parking on Main Street; and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mr. David Nash, appeared before Council to request ten
connections to water and sewr for his trailers.

On a motion by S. E. Carter, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council
voted to grant a Conditional Use Permit to Fred Jones to
set up a temporary Concrete Batch Plant off State Route 24
for the duration of the Highway By~Pass Project, that the
materials will enter the site from Highway Route 24 and

the finished product will leave by Route 1012 going
northeast to the By-Pass.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Carson-aye Hubbard-aye
Mitchell-aye Motion carried.

David Garrett, Superintendent of Public Works, presented
a report on water usuage. Council discussed this.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council
voted to deny the Conditional Use Permit to Mrs. Opal Parrish,
under Section 15-27 of the Town Code and all those

present voting aye. Motion carried.
