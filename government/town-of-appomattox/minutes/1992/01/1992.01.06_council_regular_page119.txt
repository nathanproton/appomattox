Council Minutes - January 6, 1992

On a motion by Mr. Carson, Seconded by Marvin Mitchell,

Council voted to request the Highway Department's approval of
two Handicapped Parking spaces on Main Street one at Farmers
National Bank and one at Times-Virginian Printing and all those
present voting. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to re-wire the Depot, put the office space at the front
entrance from the ramp with the restrooms and leave the
display area open to preserve the area as it is.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Carson-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye
Mitchell-aye Motion carried.

Mayor Spiggle reported the easements for the National

Park Service Line will be $4,000.00 for the Johnson/Patterson
Property.

There being no further business to come before Council, the

meeting adjourned.
