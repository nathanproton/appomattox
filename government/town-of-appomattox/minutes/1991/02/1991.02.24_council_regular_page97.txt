On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
decided not to install a Fire Alarm System at the Town
Municipal Building: arid All°-those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Bass; Council
voted to have a Street Light installed on Oak Lane in the
vicinity of House's Trailer Park, and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted to authorize the Mayor, Mr. Bass, and David Garrett,
to obtain bids on a high pressure sewer cleaning machine
and purchase said machine at a not-to-exceed figure of
$20,000.00, and all those present voting aye. Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carson,
Council voted to purchase the 1987 Chevrolet Car from
the Sheriff's Department at a price of $2000.00 and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to adopt the following resolution honoring the
men and women who are serving in the Armed Forces:

WHEREAS, the US Armed Forces of the United States of
America are presently in a position of peril
in the Middle Rast; and

WHEREAS, they are also engaged in an attitude of support
for the Desert Storm personnel, no matter where
in the world they are serving; and

WHEREAS, there are citizens of the County of Appomattox
presently serving in these United States Armed
Forces who may have their live in imminent
danger; and

WHEREAS, these service personnel_do in_most cases. have — .
families presently living in the County of
Appomattox who need and deserve as much moral
Support as possible;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Appomattox County Board of Supervisors
does hereby designate the week of February 18

through February 24, 1991, as Support Desert
Storm Week.

Bass-~aye Carson-aye Carter~aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye
Motion carried.
