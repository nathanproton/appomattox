January 14, 1991

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Carter, Council
voted to participate in the Virginians to Virginians" program
from the Virginia Municipal League and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Carson, Council
voted that the building known as J. Robert Jamerson

Library Building connection fees be waived since it is a

joint project of the Town and County, but that cost of Materials
for the connection the Town would expect to be reimbursed for.

Bass~aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Carson-aye Hubbard-aye
Mitchell-aye Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council
the meeting adjourned.
