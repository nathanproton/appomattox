1  Appomattox Town Council

Special Call Council Meeting
December 21, 2021

The Appomattox Town Council held a Special Call Council meeting on Monday, December 21,
2021 at 6:30 p.m. at the Appomattox Municipal Building, 210 Linden Street, Appomattox,
Virginia with Mayor Conner, presiding.

Town Council members present were James J. Boyce, Sr., Mary Lou Spiggle, Timothy W.
Garrett, Claudia G. Puckette.

Others present were Chad Hodges, Hurt & Proffitt, Inc., Jeff Elder, Facilities Director, Kim Ray,
Treasurer and Roxanne W. Casto, Clerk of Council.

Mayor Conner called the Special Call meeting to order.

Chad Hodges, Hurt & Proffitt, Inc. appeared before Council to update them on the bids received
on Monday, December 20, 2021 regarding the Church Street Water Line Replacement (Materials
Only) Project.

Action:
On a motion by Ms. Spiggle, seconded by Mr. Garrett, Council voted the approval and award of
the Town of Appomattox Church Street Water Line Replacement (Materials Only) Project,
having been advertised and competitively bid to Core & Main for $400,088.60, contingent upon
approval by Rural Development.

Roll Call Vote:
Boyce – aye, Spiggle-aye, Garrett-aye, Puckette– aye.  Motion carried 4-0.

Adjournment:
On a motion by Ms. Spiggle, seconded by Mr. Garrett, Council voted to adjourn at 6:55 p.m. All
members present voting aye. Motion carried 4-0.

Roxanne W. Casto
Clerk of Council

Richard C. Conner, Mayor
