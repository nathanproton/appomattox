February 10, 1986

On a motion by Mr. Noble, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council tabled a
request from Julie Coffman, Recreation Director, for the use of the
Backroom for Adult Classes, and all those present voting aye.

Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to refer to
the Property Committee the use of the Backroom for meetings and have
guidelines drawn up for policy, and all those members present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Conner, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council referred a request
from Mr.. William Cook to purchase property at the old landfill to the
Property Committee for their study and recommendations and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mr. Conner questioned whether Mr. Charles F. P. Crawley, Jr., could
obtain sewerage on some property he owns just out of Town. Due to the
moratorium on sewer, he cannot at this time.

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Hubbard, Council in accordance
with Section 2.1-344 went into Executive Session to discuss Personnel.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Noble-aye Hubbard-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Noble, Council reconvened
into regular session and all those present were voting aye. Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted to
appoint Bobbie Mullins as Administrative Assistant for the Town of
Appomattox with the authority to execute Town Business; to appoint
Mayor Spiggle and Vice Mayor Bass as. Co-Chief Executive Officers
with Mayor Spiggle's responsibilities to the Administration and
Vice-Mayor Bass's area of responsibility to be with the Utilities.

Bass-aye Carter-aye Conner-aye Hubbard-aye Noble-aye Webb-aye
Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting
adjourned.
