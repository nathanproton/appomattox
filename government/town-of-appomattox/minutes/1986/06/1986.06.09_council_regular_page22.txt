G44 ~~ council Meeting - June 9, 1986

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Webb, Council voted to
table the request on the Library until the next regular meeting
and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

Mr. Bass reported that we need to set into motion the next
phase for the Town Shop Construction, the equipment shed.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.
