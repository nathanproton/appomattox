Council minutes - July 14, 1986

On a motion by Mr. Bass, seconded by Mr. Conner, Council voted in
accordance with Section 2.1-344 of the State Code to go into
Executive Session to discuss Personnel and all those present voting
aye. Motion carried.

Council reconvened into regular session at 9:40 P.M..

There being no further business to come before Council, the

meeting adjourned.
