content: The regular meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held in the Council Room on January 18, 1971 at 7:30 P.M. with Mayor H. B. Pack presiding.

Members present were Mrs. Calvin Robinson, T. E. Conner, Burke Guill, Ivey Holland, R. C. Hubbard, and M. L. Webb.

The minutes of the last meeting were read and approved.

Hubbard reported that the new chassis for the garbage truck has been received. He moved and Mrs. Robinson seconded: That the body be painted and lettered when sent to Richmond for repairs. Motion carried.

Mayor Pack requested Hubbard, Holland, and Conner to open the bids and award the contract to the low bidder for a pickup truck.

Hubbard moved and Conner seconded that the Town force erect a storage shed when they can get the time to work on it.

The meeting adjourned.

Signed H. B. Pack Mayor

Signed C. S. Booth Clerk
