date: 1938-05-22
content: Committee Meeting

A special committee appointed by Mayor Pack to study the telephone
ordinance met on May 11, and also May 22, to study the ordinance and
make recommendations.

A number of changes were made, and the revised copy returned
to the telephone company for their approval.

See attached copy.

A motion was made by Mr. Hubbard, seconded by Mr. Conner, to recommend
to Council that the ordinance be adopted with the revisions.

R.C. Spiggle
Chairman
