Council Minutes — October 10, 2000

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to approve the joint meeting with the Board
of Supervisors on November 27, 2000 at 6:00 PM at the
Babcock House, and all those present voting aye. Motion
carried.

The Street Committee was asked to take a look at the
Parking Downtown.

Council changed the workshop meeting date to Monday,
October 30, 2000 at 7:00 PM

There being no further business, the meeting adjourned.
