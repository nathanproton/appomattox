Council Minutes ~- May 8, 2000

Now, Therefore, Be It Resolved, that the Appomattox Town
Council reconvened in regular session and hereby certifies
that to the best of each members knowledge: Consultation
with legal Counsel about litigation or specific legal
matters was the only business discussed in the closed
session by the Appomattox Town Council.

Vote:

Ayes: Joyce Bennett, C. Lewis McDearmon, Norman Mayberry
Marvin Mitchell

Nays: None

Absent: R. L. Bass and Steve Lawson
Motion carried unanimously.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Mayberry,
Council voted to have the fence near the parking lot going
towards the Mill building owned by H. G. Lawson, and the
rail around the depot be of baked on enamel on aluminum
instead of wrought iron with a similar design, and all
those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mr. Mayberry,
Council voted to approve the change-order to Main Street
Project for a new Sidewalk on the northside of Main Street
at $3,268.20, add a planter area at the Depot, and brick
edging at Lee Street $1,782.00, and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
meeting adjourned.

Koreas) 0. shugo

Ronald C. Spiggle, Mayor
