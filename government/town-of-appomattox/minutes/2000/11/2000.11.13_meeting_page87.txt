Council Minutes - November 13, 2000

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mrs. Bennett,
Council voted to request bids for the Walton Place
Waterline Extension contingent upon the right of way
easements being obtained and all those present voting aye.
Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Carson,
Council voted to table NB4 of the Agenda until the workshop
meeting and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mrs. Bennett, seconded by C. Lewis
McDearmon, Council voted to approve Phase 1, Historical
Zoning Ordinance, and all those present voting aye. Motion
carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson,
Council voted to go with Mid-State Toter roll out carts,
purchase three arms for the trucks, and all those present
voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Mayberry,
Council voted to accept the proposal of the Utility
Committee for the water and sewer rates, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.
