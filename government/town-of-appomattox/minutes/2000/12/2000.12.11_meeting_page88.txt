Council Minutes - December 11, 2000

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Lawson, Council voted to send a letter

of opposition to the proposed merger of United Airlines and U S Airways, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mrs. Bennett, Council voted to table the
request for the Town to adopt a Resolution with regard to 911 for Appomattox until
more information can be obtained and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

David Garrett, Jr., Town Manager gave a brief report of the activities of the Town.
There being no further business to come before Council, the meeting adjourned.
