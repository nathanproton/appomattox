Council Minutes - June 12, 2000

Earl Dickerson, Hurt & Proffitt, came to Council to give a
brief report to Council on the Downtown Project. Progress
is being made.

On a motion by Mr. Mitchell, seconded by Mrs. Bennett,
Council voted to accept the quote from Lynchburg Fence
Company, in the amount of $4200.00 for a change-order in
the Main Street project regarding fencing, and all those
present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,

Council voted to recess and reconvene until 7:00 PM on June
27, 2000 and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.
