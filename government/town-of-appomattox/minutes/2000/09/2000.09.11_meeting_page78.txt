Council Minutes - September 11, 2000

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Mayberry, Council voted to
reconsider the request by SBA for a 199 Foot Monopole on Clayton
Bryant’s property, and all those present voting aye. Motion carried.

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. McDearmon, Council approved
the request from SBA, Inc. for a Conditional Use Permit for a 199 Foot
Monopole on property owned by Clayton Bryant with the following two
conditions: providing a landscape buffer of Leland Cypress trees and
Evergreen trees around the base of the tower compound and install a
motion light detector at the base of the Tower.

Lawson-aye Bennett~aye McDearmon-aye Mayberry-aye
Carson-aye Motion carried.

Mrs. Sarah Terry from Congressman Goode’s office came to Council to
introduce herself and offer their help to Council.
