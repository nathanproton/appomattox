Workshop Meeting - September 26, 2000

On a‘motion by Mr. Mayberry, seconded by Mr. Mitchell,
Council voted to accept the addendum to the lease agreement
with the Chamber of Commerce with one change, Item 1, to
add after Lessee “without payment of Rent.”

Carson-aye Mitchell-aye Mayberry-aye
McDearmon-aye Motion carried. 4 to 0 vote

On a motion by Mr. Carson, seconded by Mr. Mayberry,
Council voted that before the lease and addendum will be
signed, the Chamber of Commerce must pay 50% of the past
due rent owed to the Town.

Carson-aye McDearmon-aye Mitchell-aye
Mayberry-aye Motion carried.

There being no further business to come before Council, the
workshop be closed.
C. MAYOR
