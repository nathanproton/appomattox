date: 1976-06-24
content: A special meeting of the Appomattox Town Council was held on Thursday June 24, 1976 at 8:00 P.M. with Mayor Pack presiding. The meeting was delayed until 8:00 O'clock until a quorum was established.

The purpose of the meeting was to consider the recommendations of the Water Committee held on June 16, 1976 to proposed Water Storage facilities for the Town of Appomattox.

After some discussion motion was made by Spiggle seconded by Hubbard to accept the recommendations of the Water Committee at their June 16, 1976 meeting.

Spiggle- Aye
Hubbard- Aye
Guill- Aye
Robinson- Aye
Motion Carried.
